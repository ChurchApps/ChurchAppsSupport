Displaying song lyrics is one of the main functions of FreeShow. Let's explore how to add a song and customize it to fit your needs. To quickly add a new song, right-click in the project window and select "New Show". While you can manually enter lyrics by clicking "Quick Lyrics", most songs are already available. Let's use "Web Search" to find an existing song. Select your preferred version of the song by clicking on it from the search results. You can now preview all the lyrics and make any necessary edits, add verses, or modify content. After reviewing the song, click on "Options". Adjust the display settings to your preference - for example, setting 4 lines per slide. Once you're satisfied, click "New Show" to create the song and add it to your library. In FreeShow, notice the Groups section. If you want to repeat a section, like adding Verse 1 at the end, simply click on it and it will append to the end of the song. To modify a verse, right-click on it and select "Edit". When editing a verse, remember that changes will apply to every instance of that verse in the show. Notice how the edit appears in all instances of Verse 1. Next, click on "Toggle Global Groups". Global groups are useful for adding breaks or common parts to songs. Click "New Layout" to create an alternative arrangement for this song. In our second layout, "Default 2", if we don't want to include Verse 6, right-click on it and select "Remove Group". You'll see that Verse 6 is now removed from Default 2 layout. However, Verse 6 remains in the original Default layout, making it easy to switch between different versions of the song for various bands or arrangements.
