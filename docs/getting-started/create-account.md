# Creating Your Account

<div class="article-intro">

Getting started with ChurchApps begins with creating your account. The process takes just a few minutes and gives you access to all ChurchApps tools, including B1 Admin, B1.church, and Lessons.church.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Before You Begin</h4>

- Have a valid email address ready for account verification
- Review the [Getting Started overview](./index.md) to understand what ChurchApps offers

</div>

## Step-by-Step Registration

1. Go to [B1.church](https://b1.church) in your web browser.
2. Click the **Register** button.
3. Enter your **first name**, **last name**, and **email address**.
4. Create a password for your account.
5. Submit the registration form.

## Confirming Your Email

After registering, you will receive a confirmation email at the address you provided.

1. Check your inbox for the confirmation email from ChurchApps.
2. Click the confirmation link in the email.
3. Your account is now verified and ready to use.

:::tip
If you do not see the confirmation email, check your spam or junk folder. The email comes from ChurchApps and may take a minute or two to arrive.
:::

## Setting Up Your Church

After confirming your email and logging in, you will have the option to either create a new church or join an existing one.

**To create a new church:**

1. Select the option to **create a new church**.
2. Enter your **church name** and basic information.
3. Complete the setup process.
4. You will be taken to the **B1 Admin dashboard** where you can begin configuring your church.

**To join an existing church:**

1. Select the option to **join an existing church**.
2. Search for your church by name.
3. Request to join. An administrator at your church will need to approve your request.

:::info
The person who creates a new church is automatically assigned the **Domain Admin** role, which grants full access to all settings and features. You can add additional administrators later from the **Roles** page.
:::

## What Comes Next

Once your church is set up, head to the [Initial Setup](./initial-setup.md) guide to configure your church's settings, branding, and team access.
