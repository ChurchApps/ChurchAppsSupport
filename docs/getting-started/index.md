# Getting Started

Welcome to ChurchApps -- free, open-source tools designed to help your church thrive. Whether you are a small congregation or a large multi-campus organization, ChurchApps provides everything you need to manage and grow your church community.

## ChurchApps Products

ChurchApps includes several products that work together:

- **B1 Admin** -- Your church management dashboard. Manage people, groups, attendance, donations, website content, and more from one central location.
- **B1.church** -- Your public church website and member portal. Members can view sermons, access directories, give online, and stay connected.
- **Lessons.church** -- Free church curriculum for Sunday school, small groups, and Bible studies. Access a growing library of lessons at no cost.
- **FreeShow** -- Free presentation software for your worship services. Display lyrics, slides, and media during your gatherings.

:::info
All ChurchApps tools are **100% free** with no limits, no premium tiers, and no hidden fees. ChurchApps is a nonprofit organization supported by donations and volunteers.
:::

## What This Documentation Covers

This documentation will walk you through everything you need to get up and running with ChurchApps. You will learn how to:

1. Create your account and register your church.
2. Configure your church's basic settings and branding.
3. Set up your team with the right roles and permissions.
4. Start using the tools to manage your congregation.
