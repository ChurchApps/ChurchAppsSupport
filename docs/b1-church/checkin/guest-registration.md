# Guest Registration

<div class="article-intro">

If you are visiting a church for the first time, you can register yourself and your family as guests using the guest registration page. This creates a record in the church's system so volunteers can check you in when you arrive.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Before You Begin</h4>

- Your church must have QR guest registration enabled
- Scan the QR code displayed at the check-in kiosk, or receive a link from a church volunteer

</div>

## How Guest Registration Works

Guest registration is designed for visitors who do not yet have an account. A QR code is displayed at the church's check-in kiosk that links to the registration page. You can also receive the link from a volunteer or greeter.

## Registering Your Family

1. **Scan the QR code** at the check-in kiosk or open the guest registration link on your phone.
2. The guest registration form appears with fields for the **primary contact**:
   - **First Name** (required)
   - **Last Name** (required)
   - **Email** (optional)
   - **Phone** (optional)
3. To add **family members**, tap **Add Family Member** and enter their first and last name. You can add up to 10 family members.
4. To remove a family member, tap the remove button next to their name.
5. Tap **Register** to submit.

## After Registering

Once you submit the form, a confirmation screen appears with the message: "Registration Complete -- You're all set! A staff member will check you in shortly."

Your registration creates records in the church database:

- A **household** is created using the primary contact's last name.
- **Person records** are created for each family member with a "Guest" membership status.
- A church volunteer or administrator can then check your family in using the [B1 Checkin](../../b1-checkin/check-in/looking-up-members) kiosk.

:::info
If your email matches an existing record in the church database, the system links to that record instead of creating a duplicate. This keeps the church's data clean.
:::

:::tip
Providing your email address helps the church follow up with you after your visit. It is optional but recommended.
:::
