# Volunteer Signup

<div class="article-intro">

Browse available volunteer positions and sign yourself up for upcoming services directly from B1.church. Positions are organized by category so you can quickly find where help is needed and join a team.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Before You Begin</h4>

- A B1.church account -- you must be logged in to sign up
- Your church administrator must have enabled self-signup on at least one position

</div>

## Browsing Available Positions

1. Navigate to the **Volunteer** page on your church's B1.church website.
2. You will see a list of upcoming service plans that have open volunteer positions.
3. Each plan shows the **plan name**, **service date**, and **service times**.

Positions are grouped by category (for example, "Band", "Hospitality", "Kids Ministry"). Each position shows:

- **Position name** -- The role you would fill (e.g., "Drummer", "Greeter", "Coffee Server").
- **Description** -- Details about the role, if provided by the administrator.
- **Available spots** -- A progress bar showing how many slots are filled and how many remain.

## Signing Up

1. Find a position you want to volunteer for.
2. Click the **Sign Up** button next to the position.
3. A confirmation message appears: "You're signed up! Thank you for volunteering."
4. The position now shows a green indicator confirming you are signed up.

:::info
You must be logged in to sign up. If you are not logged in, you will be prompted to log in first.
:::

## Removing a Signup

If your plans change and you can no longer serve:

1. Find the position you signed up for -- it will show a green "You're signed up" indicator.
2. Click the **Remove** button.
3. Confirm that you want to remove yourself from the position.
4. A message confirms: "You have been removed from this position."

## Deadlines

Your church administrator may set a signup deadline for each plan. The deadline is a set number of hours before the service date. Once the deadline passes:

- You cannot sign up for new positions.
- You cannot remove existing signups.
- A message at the top of the plan indicates that the signup deadline has passed.

## Position Capacity

Each position has a limited number of spots. When all spots are filled:

- The position shows as **Full**.
- The signup button is disabled.
- If someone removes their signup, the spot opens up again.

:::tip
Check the volunteer page regularly, especially early in the week. Popular positions fill up quickly, so signing up early gives you the best selection.
:::
