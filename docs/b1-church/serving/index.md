# Serving

<div class="article-intro">

The Serving section on B1.church lets you browse volunteer opportunities and sign up for positions at your church. In addition to viewing your assigned plans and service orders, you can now self-select positions that need to be filled.

</div>

## What You Can Do

- **Browse volunteer opportunities** -- See upcoming service plans with open positions that need volunteers.
- **Sign up for positions** -- Choose a position and sign yourself up with one click.
- **View your plans** -- See your upcoming and past [serving assignments](../plans/viewing-plans).
- **Remove signups** -- Cancel a self-signup if your plans change (before the deadline).

## How It Works

Your church administrator creates service plans and marks certain positions as available for self-signup. These positions appear on the volunteer signup page, organized by category (such as "Band", "Hospitality", or "Kids Ministry"). You can see how many spots are open for each position and sign up for the ones you are available for.

:::info
Only positions that your church administrator has enabled for self-signup appear on the volunteer page. Some positions may only be filled through direct assignment.
:::
