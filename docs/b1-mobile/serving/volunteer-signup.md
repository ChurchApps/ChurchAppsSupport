# Volunteer Signup

<div class="article-intro">

Browse available volunteer positions and sign up for upcoming services directly from B1 Mobile. See what positions need to be filled, how many spots are open, and join a team with just a few taps.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Before You Begin</h4>

- [Install B1 Mobile](../getting-started/installing) and [log in](../getting-started/logging-in) to your account
- Your church administrator must have enabled self-signup on at least one position

</div>

## Browsing Volunteer Opportunities

1. Open the **Volunteer** section from the app navigation.
2. A list of upcoming service plans with open positions appears.
3. Each plan card shows:
   - **Plan name** and **service date**
   - **Overall progress bar** showing total spots filled
   - **Status** -- number of spots still open, or "Full" if all spots are taken
   - **Position chips** showing each role and its open slot count
4. Tap **View and Sign Up** to see the full list of positions for a plan.

## Signing Up for a Position

1. Tap a plan to open its details.
2. Positions are grouped by category (for example, "Band", "Hospitality", "Kids Ministry").
3. Each position shows the role name, description, and available spots.
4. Tap **Sign Up** next to the position you want.
5. A confirmation alert appears: "You're signed up! Thank you for volunteering."
6. The position shows a green indicator confirming your signup.

:::info
You must be logged in to sign up for positions. The app will prompt you to log in if needed.
:::

## Removing a Signup

1. Find the position you signed up for -- it shows a green "You're signed up" indicator.
2. Tap **Remove**.
3. A confirmation dialog asks if you are sure.
4. Confirm to remove yourself from the position.

## Deadlines and Capacity

- **Deadlines** -- Your church may set a cutoff time before the service. After the deadline, you cannot sign up or remove signups. A banner at the top of the plan indicates when the deadline has passed.
- **Capacity** -- Each position has a limited number of spots. When full, the signup button is disabled. Spots reopen if someone cancels.

:::tip
Enable [push notifications](../community/notifications.md) to stay informed about new volunteer opportunities and changes to your assignments.
:::
