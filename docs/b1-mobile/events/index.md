# Events

<div class="article-intro">

The Events feature in B1 Mobile lets you browse upcoming church events and register directly from your phone. When your church enables registration for an event, you can sign up yourself and your family members with just a few taps.

</div>

## Finding Events with Registration

Events with registration enabled appear in your group calendars. When you tap on an event that has registration available, you will see a **Register** button in the event details.

Events may show different statuses:

- **Open** -- Registration is available and you can sign up.
- **Not Yet Open** -- The registration window has not started yet.
- **Closed** -- The registration deadline has passed.
- **Full** -- All available spots have been taken.

## What You Need

- [B1 Mobile installed](../getting-started/installing) and [logged in](../getting-started/logging-in)
- Your church must have event registration enabled for the event
