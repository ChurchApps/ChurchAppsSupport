# Lessons.church

<div class="article-intro">

Lessons.church is a completely free curriculum platform for churches. There are no subscriptions, no fees, and no limits on usage. It provides 1,100+ video-based lessons across 189+ studies from 12+ providers, covering age groups from preschool through adult.

</div>

## What You Can Do

With Lessons.church, your church can:

- **Browse content** -- Explore a growing library of programs and studies from multiple providers, organized by age group and topic.
- **Set up classrooms** -- Create classrooms that match your ministry structure (for example, preschool, elementary, youth, or adult).
- **Schedule lessons** -- Assign specific lessons to your classrooms on the dates you need them.
- **Customize for your needs** -- Adjust lesson content to fit your church's teaching style and preferences.
- **Present to classes** -- Use the built-in presenter mode to walk through lessons step by step during class time.

## How Content Is Organized

Lessons.church organizes curriculum in a clear hierarchy:

1. **Programs** -- A top-level curriculum series from a provider (for example, a children's ministry program).
2. **Studies** -- A group of related lessons within a program (for example, a four-week series on a specific topic).
3. **Lessons** -- An individual teaching session within a study, typically used for a single class meeting.
4. **Venues** -- Different versions of a lesson tailored for specific age groups or settings (for example, a preschool version and an elementary version).

## Integrations

Lessons.church works with other ChurchApps tools to help you deliver content:

- **B1.church** -- Volunteers and parents can access upcoming and recent lessons through the B1 app.
- **FreeShow** -- Use FreeShow presentation software to display lesson content on screen.
- **Venue TV App** -- Display lessons directly on a TV using the Android TV app.
