1. ![Calendar 1](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/f636d0e4-029f-45e0-99dd-74f77a0dfaa4)
   Make sure you have at least one group set up and there are people in the group. Choose one person in your group to be a group leader. The group leader is the only person who can edit the group calendar.

2. ![Calendar 2](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/d8757e00-3470-40ae-b4b9-a5d82692dfed)
   Navigate to B1.church

   Make sure a groups is set up in b1. Click on the admin icon.

4. <img width="1299" alt="Group Calendar 103" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/1caf8985-2ac0-4d4d-a6aa-e3b132576532">
   Click on the mobile icon

5. <img width="1302" alt="Group Calendar 104" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/203b1feb-9d39-4c9e-8ee9-8bc83995ab25">
   Click on the + to add a tab.

6. <img width="1300" alt="Group Calendar 105" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/d84f00cb-1663-43e8-a9b0-9b752d339366">
   Type "Groups"

7. <img width="1297" alt="Group Calendar 106" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/182e7f21-1219-4b71-82d2-af03415f28b3">
   <img width="1287" alt="Group Calendar 107" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/bc909678-a828-4194-8193-3d5c8474c26b">
   Add the group icon if you would like.

8. <img width="1301" alt="Group Calendar 108" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/a60627e1-6052-42f0-bdec-2133ff64d64d">
   <img width="1303" alt="Group Calendar 109" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/eac64539-9c42-4a06-9ffb-67ac63a4fb44">
   Choose the type "My Groups"

9. <img width="1294" alt="Group Calendar 110" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/c4e56f9e-6e43-4f9e-a7f7-ac901f7a539a">
   Be sure to click Save.

10. ![Calendar 3](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/5b1a8957-1dc1-4bee-ba6e-342854088dfa)
    Click on the hamburger icon

11. ![Calendar 4](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/d54b1c0a-bc96-4b6a-b5ef-d4a3c56da214)
    Choose groups

12. ![Calendar 5](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/6971a42f-c52d-454c-b489-58479918a9f0)
    Select one of your groups

13. ![Calendar 6](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/a998c0d3-e1cf-48ad-a1ee-2ed827f2f108)
    Click "add an event" or click on a date

14. ![Calendar 7](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/16d8d074-e32c-4dc8-a591-86c79d4b0940)
    Fill out the event form

15. ![Calendar 8](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/bf2470b0-a025-466d-8d26-0c88f4a225ab)
    Be sure to add a start and end time for the event

16. <img width="1191" alt="Calendar 8:1" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/a4e126e5-8cbc-48d6-9bec-bb1af9387bc8">
    Click the recurring box it the event repeats

17. <img width="1191" alt="Calendar 8:2" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/6a3b2632-07f3-4c7b-88fc-d401f551989c">
    Choose the Interval, Frequency and when the recurring event ends

18. ![Calendar 9](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/b6d87e03-9ffc-4207-9100-a68233293f07)
    Be sure to save your event

19. ![Calendar 10](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/735ee0c4-738b-4975-a00b-2950b4ebb12a)
    The event will show up in your group calendar

20. ![Calendar 11](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/c025009f-a99c-4700-b1da-07c2d8adf8b9)
    You can add notes to your calendar at the bottom of the page

21. ![Calendar 13](https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/e14b0464-82e0-4f79-ac5e-bb12d8d0f35d)
    The event will show up in your member portal
