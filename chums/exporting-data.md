**Exporting Data from Chums**

<img width="1272" alt="Exporting Data 1" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/72c98fd6-67ee-4ddd-b42d-a9fab9e6c9e2">

In the Chums dashboard click on the settings icon.

<img width="1272" alt="Exporting Data 2" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/9b3c724a-9719-4ac0-b309-e835cdf0543a">

Click on import/export

<img width="1269" alt="Exporting Data 3" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/308d606c-8adb-467f-bba7-aaaa458ee950">

Click the "Choose One" button

<img width="1272" alt="Exporting Data 4" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/b702310e-161e-46fe-92a5-4a4b80fbefe6">

Choose "Chums database"

<img width="1266" alt="Exporting Data 5" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/2d0970c9-13c3-4da5-9626-e518297279a4">

This will take you to "Step 2-Preview"

<img width="1265" alt="Exporting Data 6" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/21f18175-1ee8-4f31-9857-cf898396bd05">

Scroll to the bottom of that tab and click "Continue"

<img width="1273" alt="Exporting Data 7" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/a0ae708c-82b4-4b85-8bd6-adb812f431db">

This will take you to "Step 3-Destination"

<img width="1268" alt="Exporting Data 8" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/83040512-69ba-4cab-8397-015dfdcb1f79">

Click on "Chums Export Zip"

<img width="1264" alt="Exporting Data 9" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/9d314528-aa19-40b3-92f8-f39d6759e008">

This will take you to "Step 4-Run" and show progress with green check marks

<img width="1272" alt="Exporting Data 10" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/2bb3d04b-3665-4786-8e5c-b0fb96f3f3bd">

When this step is complete the zipped folder will download to your download file.

<img width="1262" alt="Exporting Data 11" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/be3d80ff-e591-4205-bcff-d5796d167853">

Find the "Chums Download" file in your downloads.

<img width="1270" alt="Exporting Data 12" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/53035f6e-e907-4466-81d0-efd5dc652eff">

If the file is not unzipped, unzip it and find the "people.csv" file.

<img width="1198" alt="Exporting Data 13" src="https://github.com/ChurchApps/ChurchAppsSupport/assets/127863068/8d4576fd-65a2-4a67-b91b-bb1f68e36a7d">

Open the "people.csv" file. It contains your data. You can now use your data with excel or numbers.
