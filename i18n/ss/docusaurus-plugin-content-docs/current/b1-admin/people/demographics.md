# Simo Sebantfu

<div class="article-intro">

I-dashboard ye-**Demographics** ikupha simo lesibonakalako selibandla lakho — kutsi bangobani, bangaki, kanye nekutsi emalunga akho ahlukaniswe njani emacebeni eminyaka, bulili, simo semshado, kanye netikhungo (campuses). Shathi ngayinye iyasebenta: chofota nayiphi indzawo kuze ugcugcutele ngco kusesho le-People lelihlungiwe lelikhombisa labo bantfu ngco.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Bantfu kufanele babe khona kuluhlu lwakho. Bona [Kwengeta Bantfu](./adding-people.md) nobe [Kungenisa Imininingwane](./importing-data.md) kuze ucale.
- Kuze ubone ishathi ye-Campus, kucala udzinga tikhungo (campuses) letihleliwe ku- [Settings → Campuses](../settings/campuses.md).

</div>

## Kuvula i-Demographics

Kusukela kulikhasi le-People, chofota inkinobho ye-**Demographics** ku-header yelikhasi (luphawu lwe-bar chart). Likhasi likhombisa inani lakho lelilonkhe labantfu etulu, kulandzelwe tishathi letichazwe ngentasi.

## Tishathi

### Kusakateka Kweminyaka

Ishathi ye-bar leyihlanganisiwe lekhombisa kutsi bangaki bantfu labakuliwundvu ngalinye leminyaka (sibonelo, 0–9, 10–17, 18–24, kuye embili kuze kufike ku-65+). Ibha ngayinye ihlukaniswe tincenye letintsatfu — **Female**, **Male**, kanye ne-**Unassigned** — kuze ukwati kubona kwehlukaniswa ngebulili ngekhatsi kwelicembu leminyaka ngalinye ngekukhangela kanye.

### Simo Selilunga

Ishathi ye-donut lehlukanisa libandla lakho ngesimo selilunga (sibonelo, Member, Regular Attender, Visitor, njalonjalo).

### Bulili

Ishathi ye-donut lekhombisa lisiko lemarekhodi ebulili besifazane, besilisa, nalanga-abelwa (unassigned) kuluhlu lwakho.

### Simo Semshado

Ishathi ye-donut lekhombisa kwehlukaniswa kwetinani tesimo semshado (sibonelo, Married, Single, Widowed).

### Campus

Ishathi ye-donut lekhombisa kutsi bantfu basakateke njani etikhungweni telibandla lakho. Lesishathi sivela kuphela nangabe unetikhungo letihleliwe futsi kufanele kube nemuntfu lomunye lohleliwe kusikhungo. Bantfu labangahlelwanga kusikhungo bavela njenge-Unassigned.

## Kugubha Ngekhatsi Kwemininingwane

Chofota nayiphi incenye yeshathi ye-donut nobe nayiphi indzawo yebha kushathi yeminyaka kuze uye kulikhasi le-People lelesengihlungiwe kuze ukhombise labo bantfu ngco. Sebentisa loku kwakha ngekushesha luhlu lwekuxhumana, kwenta sento lesibanti, nobe kukhipha incenye — sibonelo, chofota incenye ye-Visitor ku-Membership Status kuze ubone bonkhe betivakashi bese ubatfumela ku-workflow.

:::tip
Ngemuva kwekugubha ngekhatsi kwemiphumela lehlungiwe, sebentisa **Save as List** kusesho le-People kuze ugcine loko kuhlunga kusetjentiswa esikhatsini lesitako. Bona [Saved Lists](./lists.md).
:::

## Tindzaba Letihlobene

- [Campuses](../settings/campuses.md) — hlela tikhungo kuze ishathi ye-Campus ivele
