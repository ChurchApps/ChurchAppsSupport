# Kusesha nge-AI

<div class="article-intro">

Insita ye-**Kusesha nge-AI** ikuvumela kutsi usesha kuchibi lakho lelibhalisela ibandla usebentisa imibuto lelula, yendzabuko yekukhuluma esikhundleni semikhawulo yekusesha yesejwayelekile kanye netimo. Esikhundleni sekwakha umbuto ngetimo letitsite tekukhawula, ungachaza nje loku lokufunako ngelulimi lolusebentiswa nsuku nsuku -- lokwenta kubelula kuphendvula imibuto lenzima ngebandla lakho.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Udzinga i-akhawundi ye-B1 Admin lesebentako lenemvumo yekubona bantfu. Bona [Tikhundla & Timvumo](roles-permissions.md) uma ungacinisekile ngelizinga lakho lekufinyelela.
- Kwati ngetitfulo letijwayelekile te [Kusesha Bantfu](searching-people.md) kuyasita kepha akudzingekile.

</div>

## Kusebenta Njani

Kusesha nge-AI kusebentisa bukhona bengcondvo yebuchwepheshe kuchaza umbuto wakho bese kubuyisa imiphumela lefanako kusuka kuchibi lakho lebantfu. Ungabuta imibuto ngendlela lobungayibuta ngayo kumsebenti wakini.

1. Yendlela ekhasini le-**Bantfu** ku-B1 Admin.
2. Funa inketho ye-**Kusesha nge-AI** ngaphansi kwelawulo lekusesha.
3. Bhala umbuto wakho ngelulimi lolulula.
4. Cindzetela **Enter** nobe uchofoze **Search** kute usebentise umbuto wakho.
5. Ithebula lemiphumela litawukhuphuka kutfoveta bantfu labafanako.

## Tibonelo Temibuto

Nasi tibonelo tetinhlobo temibuto longayibuta:

- "Ngubani longakatfoli endzaweni etinyangeni letintsatfu letedlulile?"
- "Ngikhombise onkhe emalunga langaphansi kwemashumi lamatsatfu"
- "Ngikhombise imindeni lenebantfwana bebasha"
- "Ngubani lojoyine libandla nyalo lomunyaka?"
- "Ngikhombise wonkhe umuntfu longenayo i-imeyili"
- "Ngemalunga aphi lahlala kusigceme se-75001?"

:::tip
Yiba ngalokucacile ngangako lokungenteka embutweni wakho. Njengobe uniketa imininingwane leyengeti, kutakuba nembeni imiphumela yakho.
:::

## Nini Kusebentisa Kusesha nge-AI

Kusesha nge-AI kusita kakhulu nangabe:

- Udzinga kuhlanganisa timo letinyenti letingaba matima kutisetjenta ngemikhawulo yesejwayelekile.
- Awucinisekile kutsi ngutiphi tinhlaka ekufanele usesha ngato.
- Ufuna imphendvulo lesheshako yembuto lophatselene nebandla lakho ngaphandle kwekwakha umbiko.

:::info
Kusesha nge-AI kusebenta kanye nekusesha kwesheshako kwesejwayelekile kanye nemikhawulo yemakholomu. Sebentisa lendlela lekulungele tidzingo takho. Kwekufuna emabito lalula, [kusesha kwesheshako](searching-people.md) kuyintfo lesheshako. Kwemibuto lecacile kakhulu, Kusesha nge-AI kukhanya kakhulu.
:::

Kwenkhe tinhlobo tekusesha, bona [Kusesha Bantfu](./searching-people.md).
