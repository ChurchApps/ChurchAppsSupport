# Kungenisa Imininingwane

<div class="article-intro">

Lithulusi le-B1 Transfer lentela kubelula kuletsa imininingwane yakho lesele ikhona ku-B1, nome ucala kabusha ku-spreadsheet, ufuduka kusuka kuletinye tinhlelo tekuphatsa libandla, nobe ungenisa emarekhodi etipho. Lingasetjentiswa nakuphi kusikhatsi kute ukhiphe nobe wenta sipele semininingwane yakho.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Udzinga i-akhawundi ye-B1 Admin lesebentako lenekufinyelela ku-**Settings**.
- Yiba nemininingwane yakho lekhishiwe futsi ilungile kusuka kuhlelo lwakho lwangaphambilini ngaphambi kwekucala.
- Lelithulusi lentelwe kufuduka kwemininingwane kwekucala. Nangabe sewusebentise B1 sikhatsi lesidze, kungenisa kabusha kungadala emarekhodi laphindziwe.

</div>

## Kufinyelela Kulithulusi Le-Transfer

1. Ngena ku-**B1 Admin**.
2. Yendlela ku-**Settings** kubar lesesandleni sancele.
3. Chofoza inkinobho ye-**Import/Export** ngasesandleni sesekudla sesihloko sekhasi.
4. Loku kutavula lithulusi le-**B1 Transfer** kuthebhu lensha ku-[transfer.b1.church](https://transfer.b1.church).

Lithulusi le-transfer likuhambisa etinyatselweni letine: Source, Preview, Destination, ne-Run.

---

## Sinyatselo 1 - Khetsa Umtfombo Wakho

Khetsa kutsi imininingwane yakho ivela kuphi. Kukhona tinketho letisikhombisa:

- **B1 Database** — Ikhipha imininingwane cocondzana kusuka ku-B1 yebandla lakho lesele likhona. Kuyasita kwekwenta sipele nobe kuguca imininingwane yakho kuya kulolunye luhlobo. Kufanele ube ungenile kute usebentise lenketho.
- **B1 Import Zip** — I-zip yefayela ngelifomethi le-B1 lucobo. Loku kusetjentiswa kakhulu kubuyisela kukhishwa kwe-B1 kwangaphambilini.
- **Breeze Import Zip** — I-zip yefayela leneemafayela lakhishiwe kusuka ku-Breeze ChMS.
- **Planning Center Zip** — I-zip nobe ifayela le-CSV lelikhishwe kusuka ku-Planning Center.
- **Custom CSV / Excel** — Nome ngu ifayela le-CSV nobe le-Excel lelinemininingwane yebantfu. Ngemuva kwekulayisha, utakwenta i-mapha yemakholomu akho kuya etinhlakeni te-B1 ngaphambi kwekucala kwekungenisa.
- **Tithe.ly CSV** — Ifayela lebantfu nobe tipho lelikhishwe ku-Tithe.ly (kwemukelwa ifomethi le-CSV nobe le-Excel).
- **CCB / Pushpay CSV** — Ifayela le-CSV lebantfu nobe tipho lelikhishwe ku-Church Community Builder nobe Pushpay.

Ungadonsa bese ulahlela ifayela lakho endzaweni yekulayisha, nobe uchofoze kute ulisese.

---

## Sinyatselo 1b - Mapha Tinhlaka Takho (Custom CSV / Excel kuphela)

Nangabe ukhetse **Custom CSV / Excel**, ngemuva kwekulayisha ifayela lakho lithulusi litakukhombisa sikrini sekumapha tinhlaka ngaphambi kweskhatsi sekubuka sibonelo.

Ikholomu ngayinye kusuka efayeleni lakho ilotjiwe kanye nelinani lesibonelo. Kwekholomu ngayinye, sebentisa dropdown kute ukhetse inhlaka ye-B1 lefanako. Lithulusi litazikhomba emabito lajwayelekile emakholomu njenge "First Name," "Email," nobe "Zip Code," kepha kufanele uhlole umugca ngamunye bese ulungisa nome yini lelishiye.

Tinhlaka te-B1 letikhona tifaka:

- Libito Lekucala, Sibongo, Libito Lemkhatsi, Libito Lekuhlekisa, Libito Lekubonakalisa, Sihloko/Prefix, Suffix
- I-imeyili, Lucingo Lwasekhaya, Umakhalekhukhwini, Lucingo Lwasemsebentini
- Likheli 1, Likheli 2, Idolobha, Sifundza, Ikhodi Yeposi
- Lusuku Lekutalwa, Bulili, Simo Semshado, Simo Selilunga
- Libito Lemndeni/Lomndeni
- Libito Lelicembu — inikeza umuntfu licembu ngelibito
- **Impendvulo Yefomu (inhlaka lekhethekile)** — igcina linani lalelokholomu njengenhlaka lekhethekile lehlanganiswe nerekhodi lemuntfu. Nangabe usebentisa lenketho, utabutwa kutsi unikeze fomu libito.

Emakholomu longawafuni kungenisa angabekwa ku-**(Skip)**. Kufanele kube nayo inhlaka yebito lelinye lokungenani (Libito Lekucala nobe Sibongo) ngaphambi kwekutsi ukhone kucubungula.

Chofoza **Confirm Mapping & Import** kute uye ekubukeni sibonelo.

## Sinyatselo 2 - Buka Sibonelo Semininingwane Yakho

Ngemuva kwekulayisha, lithulusi likhombisa sibonelo saloko lokutakungeniswa. Sebentisa emathebhu kute ubuyeketa luhlobo lwemininingwane ngalunye:

- **Bantfu** — Balotjiwe ngemndeni, kanye netitfombe uma tifakiwe.
- **Emacembu** — Ahlelwe ngendzawo, sikhatsi, sikhatsi sedvute, kanye neluhlobo.
- **Kufika** — Emalanga esikhatsi, emacembu, kanye netibalo tekuvakasha.
- **Tipho** — Emabhentji, timali, labaniki, kanye netamale.
- **Emafomu** — Emabito emafomu kanye netinhlobo tekucuketfwe.

Buyeketa loku ngekucophelela ngaphambi kwekutchubeka. Nangabe kukhona lokubonakala kungahambi kahle, chofoza **Start Over** bese ulungisa ifayela lakho lemtfombo.

---

## Sinyatselo 3 - Khetsa Indzawo Yakho Yekuya

Khetsa kutsi imininingwane yakho ihamba iye kuphi:

- **B1 Database** — Ingenisa cocondzana ku-database ye-B1 yebandla lakho. Ngemuva kwekukhetsa loku, lithulusi litakukhombisa inani lekugcina lemarekhodi latakwengetwa. Chofoza **Start Transfer** kute ucinisekise.
- **B1 Export Zip** — Yehlisela imininingwane yakho njenge-zip yelifomethi le-B1. Kuhle kwesipele.
- **Breeze Export Zip** — Iguca imininingwane yakho ibe lifomethi le-Breeze.
- **Planning Center Zip** — Iguca imininingwane yakho ibe lifomethi le-Planning Center.

:::warning
Umtfombo kanye nendzawo yekuya akukwati kufana. Nangabe kufana, lithulusi litakuxwayisa kute kuvikelwe kuphindaphindwa lokungakafanele.
:::

---

## Sinyatselo 4 - Chubeka

Lithulusi licubungula kufudukiswa bese likhombisa inchubo yesinyatselo ngasinye:

- Emakhempasi, Tinsita, kanye Netikhatsi
- Bantfu
- Titfombe
- Emacembu kanye Nemalunga Emacembu
- Tipho
- Kufika
- Emafomu, Imibuto, Timphendvulo, kanye Nekutfunyelwa Kwemafomu
- Kuncitjiswa (kwendzawo yekuya lokuyi-zip kuphela)

:::warning
Ungavali i-browser yakho ngesikhatsi kufuduka kusachubeka. Yindla kuze tonkhe tinyatselo tikhombise njengeticedziwe.
:::

## Kulungiselela I-Breeze Import Zip

1. Ku-Breeze, yendlela ku-**Settings** bese uchofoza **Export** kubar lesesandleni sancele.
2. Khipha emafayela lamatsatfu lahlukene: **People**, **Tags**, ne-**Contributions**.
3. Khetsa onkhe emafayela lamatsatfu, chofoza ngesandla sesekudla, bese uwaphindfaphindza aphindfe abe yi-zip linye.
   - Ku-Mac: khetsa emafayela, chofoza ngesandla sesekudla, bese ukhetsa **Compress**.
   - Ku-PC: khetsa emafayela, chofoza ngesandla sesekudla, khetsa **Send to**, bese **Compressed (zipped) folder**.
4. Layisha ifayela le-zip usebentisa inketho ye-**Breeze Import Zip** kuSinyatselo 1.

Kungenisa kwe-Breeze kudlulisela bantfu, emacembu (tags), kanye nemarekhodi etipho ngekutentela.

---

## Kulungiselela Kukhishwa Kwe-Planning Center

1. Ku-Planning Center, khipha imininingwane yakho yebantfu njengefayela le-CSV nobe le-zip.
2. Yilayishe usebentisa inketho ye-**Planning Center Zip** kuSinyatselo 1.

---

## Kulungiselela Kukhishwa Kwe-Tithe.ly

1. Ku-Tithe.ly, khipha imininingwane yakho ye-**People** njengefayela le-CSV nobe le-Excel. Ungakhona futsi kukhipha ifayela le-**Giving** lelihlukene nangabe ufuna kuletsa emarekhodi etipho.
2. Lithulusi litazikhomba ngekutentela kutsi ifayela likaketse imininingwane yebantfu nobe yetipho kusekelwe emabito emakholomu.
3. Layisha ifayela usebentisa inketho ye-**Tithe.ly CSV** kuSinyatselo 1.

:::info
Kukhishwa kwe-Tithe.ly kungangeniswa ifayela linye ngesikhatsi. Yenta lenchubo kabili nangabe udzinga kungenisa bantfu kanye netipho ngekwehlukanisa.
:::

---

## Kulungiselela Kukhishwa Kwe-CCB nobe Pushpay

1. Ku-Church Community Builder nobe Pushpay, khipha imininingwane yakho ye-**People** njengefayela le-CSV. Ungakhona futsi kukhipha ifayela lelihlukene letipho/emniko.
2. Lithulusi litazikhomba ngekutentela kutsi ifayela likaketse imininingwane yebantfu nobe yetipho kusekelwe emabito emakholomu.
3. Layisha ifayela usebentisa inketho ye-**CCB / Pushpay CSV** kuSinyatselo 1.

---

## Ngemuva Kwekungenisa

Nasekucedzile kufuduka, tsatsa emaminithi lambalwa kucinisekisa imininingwane yakho:

1. Cwaninga ekhasini le-[Bantfu](../people/adding-people.md) bese uhlola tiphrofayili letimbalwa.
2. Cinisekisa kutsi emabito, ti-imeyili, tinombolo tekucingo, kanye nemakheli kufike kahle.
3. Hlola kutsi kuhlangana kwemindeni kukahle.
4. Buyeketa nome ngu emacembu kanye nemarekhodi etipho lakungenisiwe.

Nangabe ubona tinkinga, ungahlela tiphrofayili ngasinye kusuka ekhasini le-People. Ungakhona futsi kusebentisa lithulusi le-transfer kabusha kute [ukhiphe imininingwane yakho](exporting-data.md) njengesipele.
