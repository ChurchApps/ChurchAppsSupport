# Umklami Welebula Yekungena

<div class="article-intro">

Umklami Welebula ukuvumela kutsi udale futsi uhlele emathempuleti elebula legama neliphepha lekulanda lokuphrinta nemindeni nayingenisa bantfwana bayo. Ungalawula ngalokugcwele kutsi yimuphi umniningwane lovela kulebula ngayinye, kutsi ubekwe kuphi, nekutsi ubukeka njani.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Hlela [Kubakhona](setup) bese uhlela kungenani sikhatsi senkhonzo lesinye lesinekungena kuvuliwe
- Hlela [Kungena](check-in) kuze emalebula aphrinte
- Udzinga kufinyelela kwekuphatsa esigabeni sekubakhona

</div>

## Kuvula Umklami Welebula

Ku-B1 Admin, yiya ku-**Kubakhona** kulohlu lolusesandleni sancele bese ukhetsa **Emalebula**. Utawubona luhlu lwemathempuleti akho elebula lagciniwe, lehlukaniswe ngalusiko: **Lebula Legama** ne-**Liphepha Lekulanda**.

## Tinhlobo Telebula

- **Lebula Legama** — kuphriwe kunanyatiselwe kumntfwana. Ngalokuvamile ihlanganisa ligama lemntfwana, ikilasi/sifundvo sakhe, nelinombolo lekuphepha.
- **Liphepha Lekulanda** — kunikwe umtali nobe umgcini. Ngalokuvamile lihlanganisa linombolo lekuphepha neluhlu lwebantfwana labangenisiwe.

I-B1 ikucalisa nge-lebula legama lejwayelekile ne-liphepha lekulanda lelijwayelekile lesayizini lelijwayelekile lelima-3.5 × 1.1 emamashi wemalebula lashisako.

## Kudala Ithempuleti Yelebula

1. Chofota **Ngeta Lebula Legama** nobe **Ngeta Liphepha Lekulanda** (nobe usebentise luhlu lolwehlako kute ukhetse).
2. Ithempuleti lensha iyavuleka ku-editor yelebula.

### I-Editor Yelebula

I-editor ikhombisa kubuka lokulinganiselwe kwelebula ngesayizi lehleliwe. Ephasini lasesandleni sancele ungahlela:

- **Ligama** — ligama lethempuleti (kuwe wedvwa)
- **Luhlobo Lwelebula** — Lebula Legama nobe Liphepha Lekulanda
- **Bubanti / Bude** — sayizi welebula ngemamashi

### Kungeta Tikhwama

Lebula yakhiwe ngetikhwama — tincenye letehlukene tekwakheka letibekwe ku-canvas yelebula. Chofota **Ngeta Sikhwama** kute ufake sikhwama lesisha bese ukhetsa luhlobo lwaso:

- **Insimu** — itsatsa linani lemininingwane ngesikhatsi sekuphrinta:
  - `person.displayName` — ligama lelligcwele lemuntfu
  - `sessions` — inkhonzo/ikilasi labangene kuyo
  - `securityCode` — linombolo lekuphepha lelakhiwe ngekungahleleki lelekulanda
  - `children` — luhlu lwebantfwana (kumaliphepha ekulanda)
  - `person.nametagNotes` — nome ngutiphi timphawu letikhetsekile kumbhalo wemuntfu
  - `campus` — ligama lelikhampasi
- **Umbhalo** — umbhalo lomile lowubhalako (kutihloko, emalebula, nobe imiyalo)
- **Bhakhodi** — libhakhodi lelifake linombolo lekuphepha

### Kubeka Tikhwama

Sikhwama ngasinye sinemasimu e-**X**, **Y**, **Bubanti**, ne-**Bude** lachaziwe njengemaphesenti e-canvas yelebula (0–100). Lungisa loku kute ubeke umniningwane ngalokucondzile. Ungabe futsi ubeka:

- **Sayizi Yembhalo** — sayizi yembhalo ngemapoyinti
- **Kucinile** — vula/vala umbhalo lokhulu
- **Kuhlelwa** — kwesandla sancele, kumkhatsi, nobe kwesekudla kwembhalo
- **Simo** — kwekukhetsa fihla sikhwama nangabe insimu ingenalutfo (sibonelo, khombisa nametagNotes kuphela nangabe kunelinani)

### Kugcina

Chofota **Gcina** kute ugcine ithempuleti. Ithempuleti lelibuyekeziwe litawusetjentiswa ngesikhatsi lesitako emalebula naphrintwa ku-B1 Checkin.

## Kuhlela Buchwepheshe Bemathempuleti

Nangabe unemathempuleti lamanyenti elebula legama nobe liphepha lekulanda, i-B1 Checkin itawusebentisa ithempuleti lekucala kuluhlu ngekwehluleka. Hudula emathempuleti kute uwahlele kabusha.

## Kususa Ithempuleti

Chofota luphawu lwekususa kumgca wanoma ngusiphi lithempuleti bese ucinisekisa. Kususa ithempuleti lekugcina lohlobo luyibuyisela ithempuleti lelakhelwe ngekhatsi lelijwayelekile.

:::tip
Yenta luphrinto lwekuhlola ngemuva kwekuhlela ithempuleti kute ucinisekise kutsi indlela yekwakheka ibukeka kahle ngaphambi kwenkhonzo yakho lelandzelako.
:::
