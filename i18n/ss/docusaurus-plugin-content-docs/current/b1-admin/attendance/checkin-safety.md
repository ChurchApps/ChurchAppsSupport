# Kuphepha Kwekungena

<div class="article-intro">

I-B1 ihlanganisa luhlu lwekulawula kuphepha kwebantfwana ekungeneni: emilinganiso yendzawo yemakamelo nemyinge webasebenti bekuzitandzela kubantfwana, indzaba yeminyaka nesigaba se-kiosk, tinhlobo tekungena letehlukanisa emalunga, tivakashi, nebasebenti bekuzitandzela, neluhlu lwebantfu labatsembekile bekulanda ngemndeni lokutfolakala kucinisekiswa nangesikhatsi sekuphuma. Lelikhasi lihlanganisa indlela yekuhlela luhlobo ngalunye lwekuphepha ku-B1 Admin.

</div>

## Sikhwama Selikamelo Nekuvala Likamelo

Likamelo ngalinye lekungena (liqembu) lingasebentisa imilinganiso yalo. Vula liqembu, chofota **luphawu lwepensele** kute uhlele emasethingi alo, bese ufuna sigaba se-**Sikhwama Sekungena**:

- **Sikhwama** -- Linani lelikhulu lebantfu labangangenisa kulelikamelo ngasikhatsi sinye. Nakugcwele likamelo, kungena kulo kuvinjiwe futsi i-kiosk isho ligama lelikamelo leligcwele.
- **Sikhwama Setivakashi** -- Umlinganiso lohlukile lokwekukhetsa wetivakashi letingangeniswa likamelo.
- **Kuvaliwe Kungena** -- Beka ku-**Yebo** kute uvale konkhe kungena kulelikamelo ngekushesha (sibonelo, nasi-khanselwe ikilasi nobe likamelo lingatfolakali). Kuphuma kuyachubeka kusebenta.

## Emyinge Yebasebenti Bekuzitandzela

Lesigaba lesifananako se-**Sikhwama Sekungena** kuliqembu sihlanganisa imitsetfo yebasebenti:

- **Bantfwana Ngamunye Umsebenti Wekuzitandzela** -- Linani lelikhulu lebantfwana lomsebenti wekuzitandzela ngamunye longawubona (sib. 5 kusho umsebenti munye kubantfwana labasihlanu).
- **Umyinge Lomncane Webasebenti Bekuzitandzela** -- Linani lelincane kunako lebasebenti bekuzitandzela lokufanele bangene ngaphambi kwekutsi bantfwana bangene kulelikamelo.

Basebenti bekuzitandzela babalwa kuletimtsetfo nabangena ngeluhlobo lwe-**Umsebenti Wekuzitandzela** ku-kiosk (bona [Tinhlobo Tekungena](#check-in-types) ngentasi).

### Kukhetsa Kuxwayisa nobe Kuvimba

Indlela imyinge lephocelelwa ngayo kuyisethingi lebandla lonkhe:

1. Ku-B1 Admin, yiya ku-**Emasethingi > Phatsa Libandla** bese uvula ithayela ye-**Kungena**.
2. Beka **Kuphocelelwa Kwemyinge Wekuzitandzela**:
   - **Xwayisa (vumela ngekucinisekisa)** -- I-kiosk ikhombisa sixwayiso nakukhona lokungetiwe kumyinge nobe ngaphansi kwebasebenti bekuzitandzela labancane, futsi umsebenti angacinisekisa kutsi kuchubeke nome kunjalo. Loku kusetintfo lesivamile.
   - **Vimba (vimbela kungena)** -- Kungena kulelikamelo kuyalwa kuze basebenti bekuzitandzela labenele bangene.

:::info
Sikhwama ne-Kuvaliwe Kungena kuhlala kuyimilinganiso lecinile — inketfo yekuxwayisa/kuvimba isebenta emyingeni webasebenti bekuzitandzela kuphela.
:::

## Tinhlobo Tekungena

Kungena ngakunye kubhala kutsi umuntfu ung-**Lilunga**, **Sivakashi**, nobe **Umsebenti Wekuzitandzela**. Luhlobo lukhetswa ngema-chip esikrini semndeni se-kiosk (Lilunga luhlobo lolujwayelekile). Tinhlobo tisita imitsetfo yekuphepha — basebenti bekuzitandzela banika sikhwama semyinge, futsi tivakashi tibalwa kusikhwama setivakashi selikamelo.

## Kucondzisa Kwelikamelo Ngeminyaka Nangesigaba

Ungapha likamelo ngalinye emilinganiso yeminyaka nobe sigaba kuze i-kiosk icondzise imindeni emakamelweni lafanele:

- Kuemasethingi eliqembu, sebentisa sigaba se-**Iminyaka & Sigaba** kute ubeke iminyaka lencane/lekhulu (eminyakeni netinyanga) nobe/nesigaba selikamelo.
- Ku-kiosk, emakamelo lomntfwana afanelekele agcizelelwe futsi lawo langafaneleki abe mnyama kancane. Likamelo lelimnyama kancane lisengakhetswa ngekucinisekiswa ngumsebenti — kucondzisa akuvimbi ngekucinile.

Tigaba tiyaphindzaphindzeka ngelusuku lwebandla lakho lwe-**kuntjulwa kwesigaba**:

1. Ku-B1 Admin, yiya ku-**Emasethingi > Phatsa Libandla** bese uvula ithayela yekuntjulwa kwesigaba.
2. Beka inyanga nelilanga libandla lakho lentjula ngalo bafundzi (sibonelo, August 1). Iminyaka netigaba ku-kiosk kubalwa kusukela ngelusuku lwekuntjulwa lolusandza kwenteka.

## Bantfu Labatsembekile Nalabangavunyelwe Kulanda

Umndeni ngamunye ungaba neluhlu lwebantfu labavunyelwe — nobe labangavunyelwe — kulanda bantfwana bawo.

1. Vula likhasi lemuntfu ku-**Bantfu** bese ufuna ikhadi le-**Kulanda**.
2. Chofota **Ngeta**. Funa umuntfu lokhona kadze, nobe ungete umuntfu longekho ohlelweni ngekufaka **Ligama** lakhe, **Buhlobo**, nesitfombe.
3. Beka **Simo**:
   - **Utsembekile** -- Nakuphuma, lomuntfu uvela njengeikhadi lelingachofotwa lelinesitfombe sakhe, lokwenta kulanda lokucinisekisiwe kube sheshako.
   - **Akavunyelwe** -- Nangabe umuntfu atama kulanda ngalelogama, i-kiosk ivimbela kuphuma ngesixwayiso. Umsebenti angadlula loko, futsi kudlula loko kubhalwa embhalweni wekubakhona.

Chofota inkhinombho yesimo semuntfu kuikhadi kute ushintjse emkhatsini wekutsembeka nekungavunyelwa.

:::tip
Ngeta titfombe kubantfu labatsembekile bekulanda nangabe kungenteka — sikrini sekuphuma sikhombisa sitfombe kuze basebenti bekuzitandzela bakhone kucinisekisa ngeliso umuntfu lome phambi kwabo.
:::

## Kubita Umtali Nekusakata Sitiso Sesiphuthumako

Tonkhe letimphawu tembili titfumela imilayeto ye-tekisi ngemniki wetincomo we-SMS lohlanganiswe nebandla lakho — kute nsika ye-SMS lekhona ngekhatsi, ngako lomunye wemi niki labasekelekile kufanele ahlelwe kucala.

- **Kubita umtali** -- Kusuka esikrinini sekuphuma se-kiosk lesiphatswe ngumuntfu, basebenti bangatfumela umlayeto wetekisi kubatali/bagcini bemntfwana longenile (sibonelo, "Sicela wenteke enkulisa").
- **Kusakata sitiso sesiphuthumako** -- Kusuka emasethingini ekuphatsa e-kiosk, basebenti bangatfumela umlayeto kubatali bawo onkhe emndeni longenile enkhonzweni lekhetsiwe ngasikhatsi sinye. Kutfumela kudzinga kubhala **EMERGENCY** kute kucinisekiswe.

Bantfu labangavumelanga kutfola imilayeto, nobe labangenanombolo yeselula ebhalisiwe, bayeqwa ngekutigcinela — i-kiosk ibika kutsi imilayeto lemingaki lestfunyelwe nemingaki leyeqiwe.

Bona luhambo lolusuka nge-kiosk ku-[Kuphuma & Kuphepha Kwemntfwana](../../b1-checkin/check-in/checking-out).
