# Landzelela Kubakhona Kwelibandla

<div class="article-intro">

Hlela kulandzelelwa kwekubakhona kwetinkonzo temacembu akho — kusukela ekuhleleni emakhempasi netikhatsi tenkonzo, kuya ekubhaleni kubakhona ngesandla nobe nge-app yekungena ngekutimela, kuya ekubukeni tinjani kwimibiko. Nasekugcineni, utaba nemfanekiso lophelele wekutsi ngubani lokhona nanini.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngembi Kwekucala</h4>

- I-akhawunti ye-B1 Admin lenekufinyelela kwemphatsi
- [Bantfu labafakwe kuluhlu lwakho](../people/adding-people.md)
- [Emacembu lakhiwe](../groups/creating-groups.md) kwemisebenti lofuna kuyilandzelela

</div>

## Sinyatselo 1: Hlela Emakhempasi & Tinkonzo

Chaza kutsi kuphi nanini libandla lakho lihlangana.

Landzela umhlahlandlela we-[Kuhlela Kubakhona](../attendance/setup.md) kute:

1. Faka likhempasi lakho (sib., "Likhempasi Lelikhulu")
2. Faka tikhatsi tenkonzo (sib., "LiSontfo 9:00 Ekuseni", "LiSontfo 11:00 Ekuseni", "NgoLwesitsatfu 7:00 Kusihlwa")
3. Nika emacembu kusikhatsi senkonzo ngasinye kute uchaze kutsi ngutiphi emakilasi/imisebenti lehlangana ngesikhatsi saloko nkonzo

## Sinyatselo 2: Vula Kulandzelelwa Kwekubakhona Kwelicembu

Vula kulandzelelwa kwekubakhona kwemacembu lofuna kuwalandzelela.

Landzela umhlahlandlela we-[Kudala Emacembu](../groups/creating-groups.md) kute:

1. Lungisa licembu ngalinye lofuna kulilandzelela bese uvula "Landzelela Kubakhona"
2. Hlanganisa emacembu netikhatsi tenkonzo kute kubakhona kubhalwe kunkonzo lefanele

:::info
Akusiwo onkhe emacembu ladzinga kulandzelelwa kwekubakhona. Kuvule kumacembu lofuna kulandzelela kubambeka kwabo — emakilasi ebantfwana, emacembu lamancane, sikolwa seliSontfo, njalonjalo.
:::

## Sinyatselo 3: Bhala Kubakhona Ngesandla

Hlolisa kutsi ngubani lobekhona kunkonzo nakulicembu ngalinye.

Landzela umhlahlandlela we-[Kulandzelela Kubakhona](../attendance/tracking-attendance.md) kute:

1. Yiya kuKubakhona, khetsa lusuku nenkonzo
2. Hlolisa kutsi ngubani lobekhona kulicembu ngalinye

:::tip
Kubhala ngesandla kusebenta kahle kakhulu kutinkonzo tebantfu labadzala, emacembu lamancane, nanome ngukuphi simo lapho i-kiosk yekungena ingasetjenti khona.
:::

## Sinyatselo 4: Setinga Kungena Ngekutimela (Kungakhetseka)

Vumela emalunga kutingenisa asebentisa i-tablet ye-kiosk.

Landzela umhlahlandlela we-[Kungena](../attendance/check-in.md) kute:

1. Faka i-app ye-B1 Checkin ku-tablet kwekungena ngekutimela
2. Emalunga asesha libito labo bese atingenisa kanye nemndeni wabo
3. Emarekhodi ekubakhona ekungena avela kanyekanye nemarekhodi lafakwe ngesandla — akukho mehluko kudatha

:::info
Kwekuhlela lokugcwele kwemsebenti webantfwana lohlanganisa emathulusi kanye nekuhlela iphrintha, buka [Umhlahlandlela Wekungena Kwebantfwana](./childrens-checkin.md).
:::

## Sinyatselo 5: Buka Imibiko Yekubakhona

Bona simo lesibanti sekubambeka kulo lonkhe libandla lakho.

Landzela umhlahlandlela we-[Imibiko Yekubakhona](../reports/attendance-reports.md). Tibukelo letintsatfu temibiko tiyatfolakala:

1. **Sinjani Sekubakhona** — buka kubakhona esikhatsini kute ubone kukhula nobe kwehla
2. **Kubakhona Kwelicembu** — cabangela emitamba emacembeni
3. **Kubakhona Kwelicembu Ngelilanga** — kwehlukaniswa kwelilanga nelilanga kwekuhlatiya lokujulile

:::info
Imibiko ihlanganisa kokubili kubakhona lokubhalwe ngesandla nekungena ngekutimela. Akukho mehluko kutsi datha ibonakala njani — tindlela tobabili tigcwalisa emibikweni lefanako.
:::

## Usucedzile!

Kulandzelelwa kwekubakhona kwakho sekuhleliwe. Nobe ubhala kubakhona ngesandla, usebentisa i-app yekungena, nobe kokubili — yonkhe idatha ingena emibikweni lefanako kute ube nemfanekiso locondzile wekubambeka kulo lonkhe libandla lakho.
