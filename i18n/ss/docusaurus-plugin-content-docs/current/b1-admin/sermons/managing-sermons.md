# Kuphatsa Tintshumayelo

<div class="article-intro">

Likhasi le-Tintshumayelo likhombisa umtsamo wakho wonkhe wetintshumayelo. Kusuka lapha ungengeta tintshumayelo letisha, uhlele tibhalo letiselokhona, futsi uhlele kucuketfwe kwakho ngeluhlu. Intshumayelo nga yinye ingaxhumana nevidiyo nome i-audio legcinwe ku-YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, nome i-URL letsite.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngembi Kwekucala</h4>

- Udzinga imvumo **contentApi.streamingServices.edit**. Buka [Tikhundla Netimvumo](../settings/roles-permissions.md) uma ungenawo umfanelo.
- Yakha ku-[luhlu](playlists) lulodvwa okungenani kute uhlele tintshumayelo takho kulo
- Yiba nema-ID nome ema-URL evidiyo yakho lalungele kusuka ku-YouTube, Vimeo, nome Facebook

</div>

## Kubuka Umtsamo Wakho Wetintshumayelo

1. Ku-B1 Admin, chofota **Tintshumayelo** kulacala lelisesandleni sancele.
2. Likhasi le-Tintshumayelo likhombisa tonkhe tibhalo takho tetintshumayelo, tihlelwe ngeluhlu. Intshumayelo nga yinye ikhombisa sitfombe sayo lesincane, sihloko, kanye nelusuku.
3. Chofota noma ngabe ngukuphi intshumayelo kute ubone nome uhlele imniningwane yayo.

## Kwengeta Intshumayelo

1. Chofota inkhinobho **Engeta Intshumayelo** legumbini lelisekudla langetulu bese ukhetsa **Engeta Intshumayelo** kuluhlu lolwehlako.
2. Khetsa **Luhlu** lekunikeza intshumayelo kulo.
3. Khetsa **Umniki Wevidiyo** wakho -- YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, nome i-URL Letsite. Sincoma YouTube njengobe isebenta kahle kakhulu nesimo se-B1.
4. Faka i-ID yevidiyo nome i-URL bese uchofota **Tfola**. Nge-YouTube, i-ID yevidiyo yinhlanganisela yetinhlamvu leletsatfwa ngemuva kwa-`v=` ku-URL ye-YouTube.
5. Nawuchofota **Tfola**, imniningwane yentshumayelo ingeniswa ngekutentekwako, kufaka ekhatsi lusuku lwekushicelela, sikhatsi sayo, sihloko, inchazelo, kanye nesitfombe lesincane.
6. Yenta lawo lonkhe lawafunako, bese uchofota **Gcina**.

:::tip
Ungakwati futsi kwengeta i-URL yekusakaza bukhoma lehlala njalo ngekukhetsa **Engeta i-URL Yekusakaza Bukhoma Lohlala Njalo** kuluhlu lolwehlako lwe-Engeta Intshumayelo. Loku kwakha luxhumano lolungapheli kumsakazo wobukhoma weshanela yakho ye-YouTube ngekusebentisa i-ID Yeshanela yakho. Buka [Kusakaza Bukhoma](live-streaming) kute utfole imininingwane lengephi.
:::

## Kuhlela Intshumayelo

1. Chofota noma ngabe ngukuphi intshumayelo emtsamweni wakho kute uvule imniningwane yayo.
2. Buyeketa sihloko, umshumayeli, lusuku, inchazelo, sitfombe lesincane, nome tixhumanisi tekucuketfwe njengobe kudzingekile.
3. Chofota **Gcina** kute usebentise lutjintjo lwakho.

## Imniningwane Yentshumayelo

Ibhalo lentshumayelo nga linye lingafaka ekhatsi:

- **Sihloko** -- Ligama lentshumayelo lelikhonjiswa kubavakashi
- **Umshumayeli** -- Ngubani loshumayele intshumayelo
- **Lusuku** -- Lusuku lwekushicelela nome lwekushunyayelwa
- **Inchazelo** -- Sifingqo sekucuketfwe kwentshumayelo
- **Sitfombe Lesincane** -- Sitfombe sekubona lesikhonjiswa emtsamweni wakho wetintshumayelo
- **Tixhumanisi Tevidiyo/Audio** -- Ema-URL kukucuketfwe kwentshumayelo ku-YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, nome ku-akhawundi letsite

## Kuhlela Intshumayelo Kutsi Isakazwe Bukhoma

Ngemuva kwekwengeta intshumayelo, ungayihlela kutsi isakazwe ekhasini lakho lekusakaza bukhoma:

1. Hamba ethebhini ye-**Tikhatsi Tekusakaza Bukhoma**.
2. Hlela inkhonzo bese, ngaphansi kwa-**Tilungiselelo Tevidiyo**, ukhetse intshumayelo yakho eluhlwini lolwehlako.
3. Intshumayelo itawudlala ngesikhatsi lesihleliwe senkhonzo.

:::info
Kute ungenise tintshumayelo letinyenti kanyekanye esikhundleni sekutingenisa nga ngayinye, sebentisa lithulusi le-[Kungeniswa Ngebuningi](bulk-import) kute utfole emavidiyo abesuka mbamba ku-akhawundi yakho ye-YouTube nome Vimeo.
:::
