# Kuhlela Tifundvo Kusuka ku-Lessons.church

<div class="article-intro">

B1 Admin ihlanganiswe ngco ne-[Lessons.church](https://lessons.church) kute ukhone kuhlela tifundvo tetikilasi takho ngco ngekhatsi kwetinhlelo takho tenkonzo. Loku kugcina konkhe — bavolontiya, sabelo, kanye nekucuketfwe kwesifundvo — endzaweni yinye.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekutsi Ucale</h4>

- Setha imnyango yakho endzaweni yeKukhonta
- Yiba ne-akhawunti lesebentako ye-[Lessons.church](https://lessons.church) — bhalisa kucala nangabe libandla lakho lingakabi nayo

</div>

:::tip Landzela luchazo loluholako
Ufuna kubona luhlelo lonkhe kusukela ekucaleni kuze kube sekugcineni? I-**<a href="/guides/freeplay-b1admin" target="_blank">luchazo lwesinyatselo ngesinyatselo</a>** yetfu ihlanganisa kuhlanganisa bahlinzeki, kuhlela sifundvo, kanye nekuhlanganisa i-FreePlay ku-TV yekilasi lakho — kanye netividiyo letifitshane nemibhalo lonungaphindza uyihlolisise njengoba uchubeka.
:::

## Sinyatselo 1 — Hlanganisa i-Akhawunti Yakho ye-Lessons.church

Loku kusetha lokwentiwa kanye kuphela ngamunye umnyango. Kudzingeka uhlanganise i-akhawunti yakho ye-Lessons.church ngaphambi kwekutsi ukhone kubukela futsi uhlele kucuketfwe.

1. Ngena ku-[B1 Admin](https://admin.b1.church/) bese uya ku-**Kukhonta**
2. Vula umnyango lofuna kuwuhlanganisa (sib. Umnyango Wetingane)
3. Yehlela phansi uye kuligatja le-**Content Provider Accounts**
4. Cindzetela **Link New Provider**
5. Khetsa **Lessons.church** eluhlwini
6. Sikrini sekugunyatwa kwelidivayisi sitawuvela nenombolo yekugunyatwa
7. Hamba uye ku-[lessons.church](https://lessons.church), ungene, bese ufaka inombolo kute ugunyate luxhumano
8. Nasekwemukelekile, utawubona **"Account linked"** ngaphansi kwe-Lessons.church ku-B1 Admin

:::info
Ligatja le-Content Provider Accounts likhona ngamunye umnyango. Nangabe uphatsa imnyango leminyenti (sib. Wetingane kanye Nabelusha), kutodzingeka uhlanganise i-Lessons.church ngamunye ngamunye.
:::

## Sinyatselo 2 — Hlela Sifundvo

Nasekuhlanganisiwe, ungahlela tifundvo ngco kusuka ku-Tinhlelo.

1. Ku-B1 Admin, hamba uye ku-**Kukhonta → Tinhlelo**
2. Khetsa lithebhu lemnyango wakho bese ucindzetela **Add Plan Type** — nika luhlobo lweluhlelo ligama lifana neKilasi Lelibandla nobe Sikolwa SeliSontfo
3. Cindzetela luhlobo lweluhlelo losanda kulwakha bese ucindzetela **Schedule Lesson** kusifundvo sinye nobe **bulk schedule** kuluhlu lwetifundvo
3. Khetsa **lilanga** lesifundvo (lidvonswa liSontfo lelitako ngekwehluleka)
4. Cindzetela **Select Lesson** — kuvuleka liphaneli lekubukela kucuketfwe
5. Esicongweni sephaneli, cinisekisa kutsi **Lessons.church** yikhona lekhetsiwe njengemhlinzeki
6. Bukela kucuketfwe:
   - Khetsa **Luhlelo** (sib. "Bible Stories for Kids")
   - Khetsa **Sifundvo** ngaphakathi kwaloluhlelo (sib. "Creation and Early Stories")
   - Khetsa **Sifundvo** lesikhetsekile
   - Khetsa **Indzawo** — loku kuluhlelo lweminyaka yebudzala besifundvo
7. Cindzetela **Associate Lesson** kute ucinisekise
8. Khetsa **kucoshwa** kwakho kwebavolontiya:
   - **Kute lutfo** — luhlelo lolusha, kute bavolontiya labadvonswako
   - **Tikhundla Kuphela** — kucosha imisebenti yebavolontiya kusuka eluhlelweni lolwendlulile kodvwa hhayi kutsi ngubani lowabelwe
   - **Tikhundla Nesabelo** — kucosha kokubili imisebenti kanye nabavolontiya labasabelwe *(lokujwayelekile kakhulu)*
9. Cindzetela **Save**

Luhlelo lwakhiwa futsi lubitwa ngekutigcina (sib. "Feb 23 - Elementary"). Bavolontiya bangavula luhlelo kute babone sabelo sabo futsi babuyekete kucuketfwe kwesifundvo ngaphambi kweliSontfo.

:::warning
Cinisekisa kutsi ukhetse **Indzawo** lelungile yeminyaka yebudzala yekilasi lakho. Kukhetsa indzawo lengalungi kusho kutsi bavolontiya bakho batobona kucuketfwe lokwakhelwe lelinye linani leminyaka.
:::

:::tip
**Hlela ngaphambili** — Ungahlela emaviki lamaningi etifundvo kanyekanye kute licembu lakho likhone kutilungiselela ngaphambili. Sebentisa luhlu lwetifundvo letendlulile eluhlweni lweluhlelo kute ugweme kuphindza kucuketfwe ngephutsa.
:::

## Kucoshelisa Kucuketfwe Kwesifundvo

Nasekuhlelwe sifundvo, ungakulungisela ikilasi lakho lelikhetsekile — susa tigaba letingasebenti, fihla tikhundla letingasetjentiswa ligumbi lakho, nobe uhlele kabusha kucuketfwe kuze kuhambelane nendlela lofuna ngayo. Kucoshwa kungagcinwa kuphela likilasi linye nobe kusetjentiswe kuwo onkhe emakilasi elibandla lakho.

Bona luchazo lwe-[Kucosha Tifundvo](/docs/lessons-church/customization/customizing-lessons) kute utfole imiyalo yesinyatselo ngesinyatselo.

## Kudlala Tifundvo ku-TV Yekilasi nge-FreePlay

Kuhlela sifundvo ku-B1 Admin kuhambelana kahle **[ne-FreePlay](/docs/freeplay/)** — insizakalo yemabhulukhwe emavidiyo yasimahla yeChurchApps yema-TV emakilasi kanye ne-Fire Stick. Nasekusetiwe luhlelo lwakho, i-FreePlay ingakhipha kucuketfwe kwesifundvo ngco kusuka ku-Lessons.church futsi ikudlale ngesikrini lesigcwele ekilasini. Utitjhere wakho ulawula lijubane nge-remote ye-TV, achubeka etividiyweni nasesilayidini njengoba sifundvo sihamba.

Loku kusho kutsi bavolontiya bakho babona luhlelo emafownini abo ngesikhatsi kucuketfwe kudlala kuTV yegumbi — kute kusetjenta ngekwehlukene, kute ema-USB, kute kuphuthuma kwekugcina umzuzu.

[Fundza kutsi ungayihlanganisa njani i-FreePlay kumhlinzeki wekucuketfwe →](/docs/freeplay/content-providers/connecting-providers)

## Awukamboni Umhlinzeki Wakho Wesifundvo?

Luhlu lwebahlinzeki labatfolakalako luyakhula. Nangabe libandla lakho lisebentisa umhlinzeki wesifundvo longakabonakali ku-B1 Admin, sitfole bese sitosebenta kute simufakele.

Uyacelwa kukopisha futsi utfumele umlayeto lolandzelako ngco kumhlinzeki wakho wesifundvo — nasesitfolile sitowusetha luhlanganiso:

---

> **Sihloko: ChurchApps Integration Request**
>
> Sawubona [Curriculum Provider Team],
>
> Sitsandza sifundvo senu futsi sisebentisa yona njalo ngesonto netingane tetfu. Sisebentisa nge-ChurchApps kuphatsa bavolontiya betfu netinhlelo tenkonzo, futsi sisebentisa FreePlay (freeplay.church) kudlala kucuketfwe kwesifundvo ngco kuma-TV emakilasi etfu. Kube kuguqulo lokukhulu kubatitjhere betfu.
>
> Kwamanje kufanele siphatse sifundvo senu ngekwehlukene, kodvwa nangabe benihlanganisiwe ne-ChurchApps besingahlela tifundvo tenu ngco ngekhatsi kwetinhlelo tetfu tenkonzo futsi sitidlale nge-FreePlay etikrinini temakilasi etfu — konkhe ngaphandle kwekushiya emathulusi lesesiwasebentisako.
>
> ChurchApps sesihlanganisiwe nebahlinzeki labaningi besifundvo futsi licembu labo lilungele kusebenta nani nine. Ningashaya utsi nibatfintele ku **support@churchapps.org** kute kucale inkhulumiswano? Singajabula kubona loku kwenteka!
>
> Ngiyabonga!
