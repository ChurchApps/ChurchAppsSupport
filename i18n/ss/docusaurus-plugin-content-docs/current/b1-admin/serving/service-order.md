# Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo

<div class="article-intro">

Kulandzelana kwenkonzo kuchaza kulandzelana kwetintfo enkonzweni yenu yekukhulekela. Sebentisa loku kubeka konkhe kusukela engomeni yekuvula kuya kumthandazo wekuvala, kute licembu lakho lonkhe lati kutsi inkonzo ihamba njani ngaphambi kwesikhatsi.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekutsi Ucale</h4>

- [Yakha luhlelo lwenkonzo](./plans.md) lelilanga lofuna kwakhela kulandzelana kwenkonzo
- Ngekukhetsa, engeta [tingoma](./songs.md) emtapweni wakho kute ukhone kutihlanganisa ngco kulandzelana kwenkonzo

</div>

## Kufinyelela Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo

1. Hamba uye ku-**Kukhonta** bese ukhetsa umnyango wakho.
2. Cindzetela **luhlobo lweluhlelo**, bese uvula **luhlelo** lolukhetsekile.
3. Cindzetela lithebhu le-**Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo** ekhasini lelinemininingwane yeluhlelo.

## Kwakha Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo Kwakho

Kulandzelana kwenkonzo luluhlu lwetintfo letihleliwe ngekulandzelana kwato njengobe titawenteka ngesikhatsi senkonzo. Tintfo letivamile letifaka:

- Tingoma tekukhulekela
- Imithandazo
- Kufundza umBhalo
- Tatiso
- Intshumayelo nobe umlayeto
- Umnikelo
- Kuvala

## Kwengeta Tintfo Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo

1. Ku-thebhu ye-**Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo**, cindzetela **Add Item**.
2. Khetsa luhlobo lwesento lofuna kusengeta (sibonelo, ingoma kusuka emtapweni wakho, sento lesikhetsekile, nobe sihloko).
3. Faka imininingwane yaleyo ntfo, njengesihloko nanoma yimuphi umbhalo.
4. Cindzetela **Save**.

:::tip
Ungengeta tingoma ngco kusuka emtapweni wakho we-[Tingoma](./songs.md). Loku kuhlanganisa imininingwane yengoma, kufaka emavi nekuhlelwa, kuluhlelo lwakho lwenkonzo kute licembu lakho lekukhulekela libe nako konkhe lokudzingako.
:::

## Kushintjanisa Kulandzelana Kwetintfo

Donsa tintfo nge-hedlu lekusandleni sancele kwelayini ngalunye kute ushintjanise kulandzelana — yekela ku-layini lolusalubende lapho ufuna khona itfo, nobe kusihloko sesigaba kute uyifake kuleso sigaba. Kulandzelana lokukhonjiswa esikrinini kulandzelana lelicembu lakho litolilandzela ngesikhatsi senkonzo.

## Sikhatsi kanye Nemidiya

Itfo ngayinye kanye nesigaba sikhombisa kutsi kuhamba sikhatsi lesingakanani futsi kuceleni sicala nini:

- Nangabe luhlelo lunesikhatsi sinye senkonzo lesikhetsiwe, incenye yasekhohlo ikhombisa **sikhatsi sewotji** lesiciniseka itfo ngayinye lecala ngaso (sibonelo, 9:14 AM). Kungenjalo ikhombisa sikhatsi lesendlulile kusukela ekucaleni kwenkonzo.
- Tihloko tetigaba tihlanganisa sikhatsi lesihamba sonkhe lesiphakathi kwato.
- Tingoma kanye netintfo letikhetsekile tibalwa kulesi sikhatsi nasufakile **Emaminithi**/**Emasekondi** ekhasini lekuhlela itfo.
- Tintfo lotengeta kusuka emtapweni wakho wemidiya (titfombe nobe tividiyo letincane) tikhombisa **sitfombe lesincane** futsi tibalwa ngesikhatsi ngekutigcina — sikhatsi sevidiyo silinganiswa kusuka kufayela ngco, kuyilapho sitfombe sinikela sikhatsi lesilinganiselwa ~5:00 sekuhlela (sikhonjiswa nge-italics) njengobe sihlala esikrinini kuze umsebenti asikhwesise. Ungahlala uhlela nome yisiphi kuto kutsi sibe nesikhatsi lesichaziwe.

## Kuhlela Nekususa Tintfo

- Cindzetela nayiphi itfo kulandzelana kwenkonzo kute **uhlele** imininingwane yayo.
- Sebentisa incenye ye-**delete** kute ususe itfo kulokulandzelana.

:::info
Kulandzelana kwenkonzo kukhetsekile kuluhlelo ngalunye. Lushintjo lolwentako lapha luthinta kuphela luhlelo lolukhetsiwe futsi angeke lushintje letinye tinhlelo nobe umtapo wakho wetingoma.
:::

## Kufaka Tintfo Etikhatsini Letikhetsekile Tenkonzo

Nangabe luhlelo lwakho lunesikhatsi lesingaphezu kwesinye senkonzo (sibonelo, ye-8am kanye ne-10am), ungakhetsa kutsi ngiyiphi inkonzo itfo ngayinye lekufanele ibonakale kuyo. Loku kuyasita nangabe satiso sihambelana nenkonzo yinye kuphela, nobe ingoma icula kwenye inkonzo kodvwa hhayi kwenye.

1. Vula luhlelo lolunesikhatsi lesibili nobe lesengetiwe senkonzo lesichaziwe ku-thebhu ye-**Times**.
2. Ku-thebhu ye-**Kulandzelana Kwenkonzo**, cindzetela itfo kute uyihlele.
3. Ngaphansi kwe-**Include in Services**, utawubona libhokisi lekukhetsa lenkonzo nganye, lelibhalwe ngesikhatsi.
4. Susa liphawu kunoma yiphi inkonzo lapho itfo kufanele iyekwe khona.
5. Cindzetela **Save**.

Ngekwehluleka, itfo ngayinye ifakwa kuwo wonkhe inkonzo. Tikhatsi tenkonzo letingakafakwa tiyafihlwa nawuphrinta luhlelo loluhlungwe kuleyo nkonzo, kute inkonzo ngayinye itfole luhlu loluhlanzekile lolunetintfo letihlobene kuphela.

:::tip
Sigaba se-**Include in Services** sivela kuphela nangabe luhlelo lunesikhatsi lesingaphezu kwesinye senkonzo. Nangabe ubona sikhatsi sinye kuphela, hlela thebhu ye-**Times** yeluhlelo kute wengete letinye tinkonzo kucala.
:::

## Kwabelana Ngekulandzelana Kwenkonzo

Nasekucedzile kulandzelana kwenkonzo yakho, ungaphrinta luhlelo lolugcwele (kufaka kulandzelana kwenkonzo) kusuka ekhasini lelinemininingwane yeluhlelo. Loku kunika licembu lakho luhlu loluphelele lwenkonzo.

## Tinyatselo Letilandzelako

- Phatsa umtapo wakho wekukhulekela ekhasini le-[Tingoma](./songs.md)
- Wabela bavolontiya enkonzweni ku-thebhu ye-Sabelo ye-[Tinhlelo](./plans.md)
- Yakha [Imisebenti](./tasks.md) yanoma yiziphi tintfo tekulandzela letihlobene nenkonzo
