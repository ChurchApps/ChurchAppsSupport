# Kukhonta

<div class="article-intro">

Sigaba seKukhonta siyindzawo yakho lesetulu yekuhlela bavolontiya nekuhlela tinkonzo telibandla. Kungakhathaliseki kutsi uhlela inkonzo yeliSontfo, imihlangano yemkhatsi wesonto, nobe imicimbi lekhetsekile, lapha kulapho uhlanganisa konkhe kuze emacembu akho atiwe kahle kutsi ngubani lokhontako futsi ngunini.

</div>

## Indlela Lisebenta Ngayo Likhasi LekuKhonta

Nawuvula likhasi le-**Kukhonta**, utawubona imnyango yakho iboniswa njengemathebhu esicongweni. Wonkhe umnyango unetinhlobo taso letiphakeme tetinhlelo kanye nemacembu, kute ukhone kuphatsa imnyango leminyenti ngekwehlukana. Sibonelo, ungaba nemathebhu lahlukene eKuKhulekeleni, kuMnyango Wetingane, kanye neKwemukela Tivakashi.

Bacaphuni labangesibo bo-admin lababalunga wemnyango bangaphindze bafinyelele likhasi leKukhonta. Batobona kuphela imnyango labangamalunga ako futsi bangakhona kwakha kanye nekuhlela tinhlelo, imisebenti leyabelwe, tinhlobo tetinhlelo, kanye nekulandzelana kwenkonzo yemnyango wabo ngaphandle kwekudzinga emalungelo lagcwele e-admin.

:::tip
Nawungu-admin, phendukisa i-**Show All** kute ubone yonkhe imnyango kanyekanye. Loku kuyasita nawudzinga kuhlola imisebenti leyabelwe kumacembu lamaningi.
:::

## Tigaba Letincane

Sigaba seKukhonta sifaka letindzawo letibalulekile:

- **[Tinhlelo](./plans.md)** -- Yakha tinhlelo tenkonzo temilanga lekhetsekile bese wabela bavolontiya emacenjini. Tinhlelo tikusita kuhlela kutsi ngubani lokhontako futsi ngunini, kute wonkhe umuntfu atiwe indzima yakhe ngaphambi kwesikhatsi. Setha [sikhumbuto sebavolontiya](./plans.md#sikhumbuto-sebavolontiya) lesitentekayo ngekutigcina kute licembu lakho lati ngaphambi kwenkonzo ngayinye, futsi usebentise i-**[Kubuka Konkhe Kwetinhlelo](./plans-overview.md)** kute ubone yonkhe imisebenti letawenteka emavikini lamaningi kuthebula linye.
- **[Tingoma](./songs.md)** -- Phatsa umtapo wakho wetingoma tekukhulekela kanye nemavi, kuhlelwa, kanye netixhumanisi tangaphandle. Licembu lakho lekukhulekela lingasebentisa loku kutilungiselela tinkonzo letitako.
- **[Imisebenti](./tasks.md)** -- Abela futsi ulandzelele imisebenti yemalunga elicembu lakho. Setha [Kutitenzela](./automations.md) kute wakhe imisebenti lephindzaphindzako ngekutigcina, kuze kute lokushiywako.
- **[Tinchubo Temisebenti](./workflows.md)** -- Hambisa bantfu kutindzawo letilandzelanako ku-bhodi lebonakalako le-Kanban, kufaka ekhatsi kulandzelela tivakashiletisha nobe inchubo yebulunga. Tinyatselo tingasebentisa tento letitentekako ngekutigcina (kutfumela i-imeyili, kulinda emalanga lambalwa, kwengeta ecemjini), futsi emakhadi ahamba ngekusebentisa tinkhinobho temiphumela, kuhambiswa ngekusekelwa etimweni, nobe tisusi tetehlakalo netikhatsi -- kanye netincwadzi telubiko letihlangene.

## Kucala

1. Hamba uye ku-**Kukhonta** kusuka kumenyu lesikhulu ku-B1 Admin.
2. Cindzetela inkhinobho yekwengeta umnyango kute wakhe umnyango lomusha nobe uphendukise "show all" kute ubone lithebhu lemnyango lesele likhona. Khetsa i-**thebhu yemnyango** kute ubone tinhlobo taso tetinhlelo kanye nemacembu.
3. Khetsa "create a plan type" nobe khetsa luhlobo lweluhlelo lolusekhona kute ucale [kwakha tinhlelo tenkonzo](./plans.md), nobe uhlole tigaba te [Tingoma](./songs.md) kanye ne[Misebenti](./tasks.md).

:::info
Ngaphambi kwekutsi wakhe tinhlelo, kutodzingeka kutsi wakhe iMnyango yakho endzaweni yeKukhonta kanye neMacembu ngaphakathi kwawo wonkhe Mnyango.
:::
