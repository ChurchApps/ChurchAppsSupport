# Tinchubo Temisebenti

<div class="article-intro">

Tinchubo Temisebenti tihambisa bantfu ngetinyatselo letilandzelanako ku-bhodi lebonakalako. Ngamunye umuntfu uphenduka likhadi lelihamba kusuka enyatselweni yinye kuya kulenye -- kusuka ekulandzeleleni sivakashi lesisha, kuya enchubeni yebulunga, kuya ekubongeni umuntfu lonikela kwekucala, nanoma yini lapho kudzingeka ulandzelele bantfu labaningi ngendlela lefanako yetigaba. Sinyatselo singacela umvolontiya kutsi enze lulutfo (kushaya ucingo, kuba nengcoco) **futsi** singasebentisa tento letitentekako ngekutigcina -- kutfumela i-imeyili, kulinda emalanga lambalwa, kwengeta umuntfu ecemjini -- kute Tinchubo Temisebenti tiphatse kokubili kulandzelela kwebantfu kanye nemsebenti lophindzaphindzako lohambelana nawo. Tinchubo Temisebenti tandzisa [Imisebenti](./tasks.md) tibe yi-bhodi lekudonswa nekubekwa le-Kanban kute kute lokushiywako nemuntfu loshiywako.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekutsi Ucale</h4>

- Cinisekisa kutsi bantfu lofuna kubalandzelela bakhona ku-B1 Admin
- Tijwayeze kutsi [Imisebenti](./tasks.md) isebenta njani, ngobe lonkhe likhadi ku-bhodi ngumsebenti
- Kute usebentise sento se-**Send email**, kwakha kucala imifanekiso yemayimeyili lofuna kuyitfumela (kuphatswa ngaphansi kwe-**Messaging → Manage Templates**)
- Utodzinga lilungelo lelifanele leMisebenti. Kubuka, kuhlela emakhadi, kanye nekuphatsa tinchubo temisebenti kumatinga lahlukene emalungelo (bona [Tindzima & Emalungelo](../settings/roles-permissions.md))

</div>

## Kubuka Tinchubo Temisebenti

Hamba uye ku-**Kukhonta**, vula sigaba se-**Misebenti**, bese ukhetsa **Workflows** kumenyu. Utawubona tinchubo temisebenti takho tiluhlu futsi tihlanganiswe ngesigaba, kanye netinchubo letisebentako tikhonjiswa ngekugcwala. Cindzetela nayiphi inchubo yemsebenti kute uvule i-bhodi yayo.

## Kwakha Inchubo Yemsebenti

1. Kulikhasi le-Workflows, cindzetela **Add Workflow**.
2. Khetsa indlela yekucala:
   - **Inchubo lengenalutfo** -- cala ungenalutfo bese wakha tinyatselo takho.
   - **Kusuka ku-isifanekiso** -- cala ngeluhlu lolusetenteliwe lwetinyatselo longalihlela. Tifanekiso letisekelekile tifaka:
     - **New Visitor Follow-up** -- Tfumela imeyili yekwemukela → Shaya ucingo lolubuka umuntfu → Mema enyatselweni lelandzelako → Kuxhumana
     - **Membership Class** -- Bonisa intsandvo → Bhalisa ekilasini → Ya ekilasini → Cedza bulunga
     - **First-time Giver Thank-you** -- Tfumela inothi lekubonga → Yabelana ngemphumela wekunikela → Kuphatswa kahle
3. Nika inchubo yemsebenti **Ligama**.
4. Ngekukhetsa, abela **Sigaba** kute uhlanganise tinchubo temisebenti letihlobene ndzawonye. Ungakhona kwakha sigaba lesisha ngco kudropdown.
5. Shiya inchubo yemsebenti ku-**Active** kute bantfu bakhone kwengetwa kuyo, nobe uyisethe ku-**Inactive** kute uyifihle kuluhlu lwekwengeta-enchubeni-yemsebenti.
6. Cindzetela **Save**.

:::tip
Sebentisa inkhinobho ye-**Duplicate** kuluhlu lweTinchubo Temisebenti kute ukopishe inchubo yemsebenti lesele ikhona -- kufaka nemkhatsi netinyatselo tayo, tento letitentekako ngekutigcina, nekuhambisa -- njengesicalo senchubo lensha.
:::

## Kwakha i-Bhodi Ngetinyatselo

Yonkhe i-bhodi yenchubo yemsebenti yakhiwe nge-**tinyatselo**, letikhonjiswa njengemakholomu kusuka esandleni sancele kuya kudla. Vula inchubo yemsebenti bese usebentisa **Add Step** kute wakhe sigaba ngasinye senchubo yakho.

Nawengeta nobe uhlela inyatselo, ungasetha:

- **Ligama Lenyatselo** -- sihloko sekholomu (sibonelo, "Welcome Call" nobe "Awaiting Registration").
- **Kudzingeka nga (emalanga)** -- kusetha ngekutigcina lilanga lekugcina nalikhadi lingena kulenyatselo. Emakhadi lasendlulile lilanga lawo lekugcina akhonjiswa nge-**Overdue**.
- **Lowabelwe ngekwehluleka** -- umuntfu nobe licembu lelabelwa emakhadi lamasha kulenyatselo ngekutigcina.
- **Tento letitentekako ngekutigcina** -- tintfo lolusebenta lutentela lona wona nalifika likhadi (bona ngaphansi).
- **Kuhambisa** -- lapho likhadi liya khona nalishiya inyatselo (bona [Kuhambisa](#kuhambisa-emakhadi-ngemiphumela-nemibandzela)).

Donsa emakholomu etinyatselo kuze aye ekulandzelaneni lokuhambelana nenchubo yakho. Lokulandzelana futsi kusho indlela yekwehluleka lelicoshwa likhadi nakute lokunye kuhambisa lokusetjentisiwe.

:::info
Gcina inyatselo lensha kucala. Tento letitentekako ngekutigcina kanye nekuhambisa kunamatsela enyatselweni, ngako umhleli uyavula letigaba kuphela ngemuva kwekutsi inyatselo seyikhona.
:::

## Tento Letitentekako Ngekutigcina

Sinyatselo ngasinye singatfwala luhlu lwe-**tento letitentekako ngekutigcina** letisebenta wona natho ngekutigcina ngesikhatsi likhadi **lingena** enyatselweni -- ngaphambi kwekutsi nobe ngubani alithinte. Yindlela inyatselo ngayinye ecela umvolontiya *futsi* icubungula umsebenti lojwayelekile lohambelana nekulandzelela.

Ku-mhleli wenyatselo, vula **Automated actions**, cindzetela **Add Action**, khetsa luhlobo, gcwalisa timiseto tawo, bese ucindzetela lithicho lekugcina kuleso sento. Engeta ngobunyenti lobudzingekako; tisebenta **kusuka etulu kuya phansi ngekulandzelana**.

| Sento | Yentani |
|---|---|
| **Send email** | Tfumela umuntfu i-imeyili yesifanekiso losikhetsile. Ungakhona kushintja umutsi wesihloko. |
| **Wait** | Imisa likhadi emalanga lambalwa ngaphambi kwekutsi kuchubeke (bona ngaphansi). |
| **Add to group** | Engeta umuntfu ku[licembu](../groups/index.md) lolikhetsile. |
| **Add to workflow** | Cala umuntfu kulenye inchubo yemsebenti -- lokusita ekudluliseleni emkhatsini wetinchubo. |
| **Add note** | Ibhalisa inothi emlandvweni welikhadi. |
| **Set field** | Ibuyekete inkhundla emlandvweni wemuntfu: Simo Sebulunga, Simo Semshado, Bulili, Lidolobha, Sifundza, nobe i-Zip. |
| **Webhook** | Itfumela imininingwane yelikhadi ku-adresi yeliwebhu langaphandle (URL) losinikako, kute uhlanganise nalamanye emasistimu. |

Ngemuva kwekutsi tonkhe tento tenyatselo tichubekile, likhadi **liyahlala kuleyo nyatselo** kuze umuntfu alisebentise -- ngaphandle kwekutsi inyatselo inendlela yekuhamba ngekutigcina leliyihambisayo (bona [Tinyatselo letentiwa tonkhe ngekutigcina](#tinyatselo-letentiwa-tonkhe-ngekutigcina)).

:::info
Tento letitentekako ngekutigcina tisebenta kuphela nakungena likhadi ngendlela lejwayelekile -- nasengetwa kwekucala, nakuletfwa nemphumela nobe indlela yekuhamba ngekutigcina, nobe ngemuva kwekutsi i-Wait secedzile. Atisebenti kabusha nasekukhona lolisusa ngesandla asa likhadi enyatselweni nobe alibuyisela emuva, ngako umuntfu angeke atfole imeyili lefanako kabili.
:::

### Kutfumela imeyili

Khetsa **Send email**, khetsa lesinye setifanekiso takho temayimeyili, bese, ngekukhetsa, ubhala sihloko lesikhetsekile. Nalingena likhadi enyatselweni, umuntfu utfola leyo imeyili ngekutigcina. (Nangabe umuntfu angenayo i-adresi yemeyili emlandvweni wakhe, inyatselo iyeqa loku sento.)

### Kulinda emalanga lambalwa (tinchubo letinamatselene)

Sento se-**Wait** simisa likhadi ngenani lemalanga losisethile. Ngesikhatsi silindzile, likhadi likhonjiswa nge-**Snoozed**. Nasekuphelile kulinda:

1. Nobe yiziphi **tento letisele kuleyo nyatselo** tiyasebenta -- ngako ungakwakha inchubo lefana ne-**Send email → Wait 3 days → Send a reminder email**.
2. Bese, nangabe inyatselo inendlela yekuhamba ngekutigcina, likhadi liyachubeka; ngaphandle kwaloko liyahlala enyatselweni kuze umuntfu alisebentise.

:::tip
I-**Wait** ekucaleni lokugcwele kwenyatselo yindlela lelula yekumisa likhadi ngaphambi kwekutsi liye kumvolontiya -- sibonelo, *Linda emalanga lasikhombisa, bese umeluleki uyatfintana*.
:::

## Kwengeta Bantfu Njengemakhadi

Kunetindlela letiningi tekufaka bantfu ku-bhodi:

- **Kusuka ku-bhodi** -- Cindzetela **Add Card** phansi kwekholomu yenyatselo bese ukhetsa umuntfu. Ungaphindze ukhetse licembu, bese lonkhe lilunga laloo licembu lengetwa njengelikhadi.
- **Kusuka emlandvweni wemuntfu** -- Sebentisa **Add to Workflow** ekhasini lemuntfu kute umyise enchubeni yemsebenti.
- **Kusuka ekusesheni Bantfu** -- Khetsa bantfu labaningi bese usebentisa sento sebunyenti se-**Add to Workflow** kute ubengete bonkhe kanyekanye.
- **Ngekutigcina nge-sisusi** -- Engeta bantfu nakwentekile lokutsite, njengekutfunyelwa kwe-form nobe sipho sekucala (bona [Tisusi](#tisusi) ngaphansi).

## Kusebenta Ne-Bhodi

Vula inchubo yemsebenti kute ubone i-bhodi yayo. Likhadi ngalinye likhombisa ligama lemuntfu, kutsi ngubani lelabelwe kuye, kanye nelilanga lekugcina nobe simo (**Overdue** nobe **Snoozed**). Ikholomu yenyatselo iphindze ikhombise tibonakaliso letincane teto tento letitentekako ngekutigcina letisebentako kanye netincwadzi tekuhambisa kwayo, kukunika sithombe lesisheshako sekutsi emakhadi ahamba njani.

- **Susa likhadi** -- Donsa likhadi kusuka kukholomu yinye kuya kulelandzelako njengoba umuntfu achubeka.
- **Vula likhadi** -- Cindzetela kabili likhadi (nobe ulicindzetele) kute uvule idreva yalo lenemininingwane, lapho ungashintja inyatselo, uphindze wabele, wengete emanothi, futsi ubuyekete loko lokusewentekile.

Kusuka kuidreva yelikhadi ungakhona:

- **Wabela** likhadi kumuntfu nobe licembu lelihlukene.
- **Lalisa** likhadi lilanga linye, emalanga lamatsatfu, nobe liviki linye kute ufihle kwesikhashana lilanga lalo lekugcina.
- **Buyisela Emuva** enyatselweni lengaphambili nobe **Weqise** enyatselweni lelandzelako.
- **Bambelela sabelo** -- gcina umnini lofanako welikhadi noma nase liya etinyatselweni. Ngekwehluleka, kususa likhadi enyatselweni lensha kulabela kulowo lowabelwe ngekwehluleka kuleyo nyatselo; kubambelela kugcina umuntfu lofanako anesibopho ngasosonkhe sikhatsi.
- **Cedza** likhadi kute ulicedze, nobe ukhetse inkhinobho ye-**Umphumela** nangabe inyatselo inemiphumela lesethiwe (bona [Kuhambisa](#kuhambisa-emakhadi-ngemiphumela-nemibandzela)).
- **Engeta emanothi** futsi ubuyekete **umlandvo** welikhadi -- kufaka luhlu lwetento letitentekako ngekutigcina letesewentekile (imeyili letitfunyelwe, kulinda, njll.).

### Tento tebunyenti

Khetsa emabhokisi wekukhetsa emakhadini lamaningi kute usebente ngawo ndzawonye. Kuvela ibha yemathulusi lekukuvumela **kucedza**, **kulalisa**, **kuphindze wabele**, nobe **ukuse** onkhe emakhadi lakhetsiwe kuya enyatselweni lenye kanyekanye.

## Kuhambisa Emakhadi Ngemiphumela Nemibandzela

Kuhambisa kulawula lapho liya khona likhadi nalishiya inyatselo. Vula umhleli wenyatselo kute usethe tinhlobo letimbili tekuhambisa.

### Tinkhinobho temiphumela

Imiphumela tinkhinobho letikhonjiswa kuidreva yelikhadi ngesikhatsi ucedza likhadi kuleyo nyatselo. Esikhundleni senkhinobho yinye ye-**Complete**, ungakhetsa kunikwa kwetintfo letifana ne "Joined a Group" nobe "Not Interested." Umphumela ngamunye ungakhona:

- Kutfumela likhadi **kwenye inyatselo** kule nchubo yemsebenti,
- **Kudlulisela likhadi** kwenye inchubo yemsebenti ngco, nobe
- **Kuvala** likhadi.

Loku kuvumela sincumo sinye kwehlukanisa umuntfu etindleleni letihlukene.

### Kuhambisa ngekutigcina (ngekusekelwa etimweni)

Tindlela tekuhambisa ngekutigcina tisusa likhadi **ngekushesha nalingena enyatselweni** (futsi ngemuva kwekutsi tento takhona letitentekako ngekutigcina tichubekile), ngaphandle kwalobani lokuchofoztako, nangabe umuntfu ahambelana nemibandzela lebekiwe. Engeta indlela, khetsa inyatselo lekhonjiswe, bese uchaza umbandzela munye nobe lomunyenti (sibonelo, ikhampasi yemuntfu, budzala, nobe simo sebulunga). Indlela lengenambandzela ihambelana nawo wonkhe umuntfu.

:::info
Ku-bhodi, ikholomu yenyatselo ngayinye ikhombisa tincwadzi letincane letichaza kuhambisa kwayo -- sibonelo, ligama lemphumela nobe "if matches" kulandzelwa emhlweni loya enyatselweni lekhonjiswe nobe enchubeni yemsebenti.
:::

## Tinyatselo Letentiwa Tonkhe Ngekutigcina

Ungakwenta inyatselo isebente yodwa ngekwayo, ngaphandle kwalobani loyisebentisako. Nika inyatselo **tento tayo letitentekako ngekutigcina** bese wengeta **indlela yekuhamba ngekutigcina** (ngaphandle kwemibandzela) leyikhomba enyatselweni lelandzelako. Nalingena likhadi, tento tiyasebenta, bese indlela iyalichubeka masinyane -- likhadi lidzabula ngco.

:::tip
Hlanganisa loku ne-**Wait**: *Tfumela imeyili yekwemukela → Linda emalanga lamatsatfu → chubeka ngekutigcina uye enyatselweni ye-"Personal call".* Imeyili nesikhatsi kuphatswa ngawe, futsi umvolontiya ubona likhadi kuphela nasekuyisikhatsi sekuthintana nemuntfu.
:::

## Tisusi

Tisusi tengeta bantfu enchubeni yemsebenti ngekutigcina nakwentekile lokutsite, ngako awudzingi kwengeta emakhadi ngesandla. Ku-bhodi yenchubo yemsebenti, cindzetela lithebhu le-**Triggers**, bese **Add Trigger**. Kunetinhlobo letimbili:

### Tisusi Tetehlakalo

Tisebenta ngekushesha nalishintja umlandvo ku-B1. Khetsa sento, bese ngekukhetsa wengete **mibandzela** kute kube bantfu labahambelana kuphela labengetwako:

- **Person · Created / Updated** -- sib. engeta nobani lesimo sakhe sesishintjela ku-*Sivakashi*.
- **Donation · Created** -- sib. engeta sipho sekucala nobe lesikhulu kwinchubo yemsebenti yekubonga (hambelanisa ngemali, sikhwama, nobe indlela).
- **Group · Member Joined** / **Group · Created**.
- **Form · Submitted** -- engeta nobani lotfumela i-form lekhetsiwe (kuhle kakhulu ku-"I'm New" nobe likhadi le-"Connect").

### Tisusi Tesikhatsi

Sisebenta ngekulandzelana lokuphindzaphindzako -- ngelilanga, ngeliviki, ngenyanga, nobe ngemnyaka -- kucondzana nemibandzela lebekiwe. Sebentisa letu kutewulisa lokusekelwe etikhatsini njengobonkhe umuntfu lomlandvo wakhe wesikhatsi selunyaka selibulunga sinamuhla nobe kuhlolwa **kwenyanga**.

Ku-sisusi ngasinye ungaphindze usethe:

- **Inyatselo yekungena** likhadi lelisha licala kuyo (kwehluleka enyatselweni yekucala).
- **Kanye ngamunye umuntfu** -- kuze umuntfu lofanako angenjatwa kabili enchubeni yemsebenti sisusi.
- **Active** -- vula nobe ucise sisusi ngaphandle kwekusisusa.

:::tip
Hlanganisa sisusi se-**Form · Submitted** ne-isifanekiso se-**New Visitor Follow-up** kute uphendule likhadi lakho le-"Connect Card" nobe "I'm New" ibe ngumjovo wekulandzelela lotentekako ngekutigcina.
:::

## Emakhadi Ami

Bavolontiya nebasebenti abadzingi kugcuma kuwo wonkhe bhodi kute batfole umsebenti wabo. Likhasi le-**My Cards** (lelixhunywe kusuka kulikhasi le-Workflows) liluhlu lwalo lonkhe likhadi lelabelwe kumsebentisi wanyalo kuwo onkhe tinchubo temisebenti. Kucindzetela likhadi kuvula bhodi lelikhona kuyo.

## Tincwadzi Telubiko

Vula inchubo yemsebenti bese ucindzetela **Reports** kute ubone kuhlatiywa kwaloko nchubo yemsebenti:

- **Overdue** -- inani lemakhadi lasendlule lilanga lawo lekugcina.
- **Cards per Step** -- emakhadi lamaningi kangaki khona kwamanje kusinyatselo ngayinye, kukhonjiswa njengesheji sekholomu.
- **Completed (30 days)** -- kuhamba kwemisebenti emalangeni langu-30 lendlulile, kukhonjiswa njengesheji selayini.

Sebentisa lena kute utfole tindzawo letivimbako -- sibonelo, inyatselo lapho emakhadi acinana khona angaze achubeke.
