# Kubuka Konkhe Kwetinhlelo

<div class="article-intro">

Kubuka Konkhe Kwetinhlelo kukunika sibonakalo lesisetulu sesabelo sonkhe sebavolontiya bakho emalangeni lamaningi enkonzo kanyekanye. Esikhundleni sekuvula luhlelo ngalunye ngekwehlukana, ungabona kutsi ngubani lokhontako kusikhundla ngasinye emavikini latako kuthebula linye — futsi ubone masinyane nobe yikuphi kungakagcwaliswa lokusadzinga kugcwaliswa.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekutsi Ucale</h4>

- Yakha Umnyango munye kanye Neluhlobo Lweluhlelo endzaweni yeKukhonta
- Yakha [tinhlelo tenkonzo](./plans.md) ngemilanga kanye nesabelo sebavolontiya
- Cinisekisa kutsi bavolontiya bakho sebengetiwe ku-[luhlu lwebantfu](../people/adding-people.md)

</div>

## Kufinyelela Kubuka Konkhe

1. Hamba uye ku-**Kukhonta** kusuka kumenyu lesikhulu ku-B1 Admin.
2. Khetsa **lithebhu lemnyango** esicongweni sekhasi.
3. Cindzetela **luhlobo lweluhlelo** kute uvule luhlu lwaso lwetinhlelo.
4. Cindzetela inkhinobho ye-**Overview** eceleni kwesicongo sekhasi.

## Kufundza Ithebula Lekubuka Konkhe

Kubuka konkhe kukhombisa ithebula lapho:

- **Emalayini** amele sikhundla ngasinye (sib. "Music: Guitar", "Tech: Projection") ahlanganiswe ngesigaba
- **Emakholomu** amele emalanga enkonzo latako (sib. "Apr 14", "Apr 21")
- **Emabhokisi** akhombisa ligama lemvolontiya lowabelwe kuleso sikhundla ngaleli langa

Emabhokisi lakhonjiswe nge-**libovu** akakagcwaliswa — akukho mvolontiya losewabelwe. Loku kwenta kube lula kubona tikhala tebasebenti ngekushesha ngaphandle kwekuvula luhlelo ngalunye ngekwehlukana.

:::tip
Emagama ebavolontiya akhonjiswa ngendlela lefitsanyisiwe (ligama lekucala kanye nesiciniso semagama sesibongo, sib. "John D.") kute ithebula lihlale linciphile nawunetikhundla letinyenti.
:::

## Kwabela Bavolontiya Ngco Kusuka Kubuka Konkhe

Awudzingi kuvula tinhlelo ngekwehlukana kute ugcwalise tikhala letingakagcwaliswa. Cindzetela nayiphi ibhokisi kuthebula kute uvule iphaneli yesabelo yaleso sikhundla nelilanga. Kusuka lapho ungakhona:

- Khetsa umuntfu kusuka elicemjini lakho kute umwabele kuleso sikhundla
- Cindzetela **Remove** eceleni kwalobani losewabelwe kute umsuse kuleso sikhala
- Sindza lushintjo ngaphandle kwekushiya kubuka konkhe

Loku kwenta kukhonakale kufaka bantfu kuwo wonkhe luhlelo ngekuhamba kanye — sebenta emavikini nasetikhundleni ngaphandle kwekungena nekuphuma etinhlelweni ngekwehlukana.

## Kuhlela Ngekutigcina Kusuka Kubuka Konkhe

Cindzetela **Auto-Schedule** kute wente i-B1 igcwalise tonkhe tikhundla letivulekile, letingakagcwaliswa etheubuleni lanyalo kanyekanye. Kuluhlelo ngalunye lolubonakalako, ikhipha bantfu kulelicembu lelihlotjaniswe nesikhundla ngasinye bese igcwalisa tikhala letingenalutfo ngekutigcina, ibike kutsi ingaki emalungiswe kutinhlelo letibonakalako leyikhonile kuwagcwalisa. Tinhlelo tigcwaliswa ngakunye kanye kanye kute umvolontiya lofanako angasabelwa kabili emalangeni lamabili kulokwenteka kunye.

:::info
Auto-Schedule igcwalisa kuphela tikhala lesele tingenalutfo — akusuki nakanye kubaniniko lesele babelwe.
:::

## Kutfumela I-imeyili Kubo Bonkhe Labasabelwe

Cindzetela **Email Volunteers** kute utfumele satiso kuwo wonkhe umvolontiya losabelwe kunoma yikuphi kufingqi lwemilanga kanye nemnyango losahlungiwe kwamanje, ngesento sinye, esikhundleni sekutfumela luhlelo ngalunye ngekwehlukana. I-B1 ibika kutsi kuningi emayimeyili langakanani latfunyelwe futsi angakanani lehlulekile.

## Kukhombisa Luhlelo Lwemvolontiya

Sebentisa i-dropdown ye-**Highlight** kute ukhetse umuntfu kulicembu lakho — ibhokisi ngayinye lasabelwe kuyo ikhonjiswa kuthebula, kute ubone konkhe lapho asebentako khona ngaphambi kwekumengeta kwesinye sikhundla. Khetsa **Everyone** kute ucise loku kukhombisa.

## Kuhlunga Kubuka Konkhe

Ungashintjanisa loko lokukhonjiswa kubuka konkhe usebentisa emalawuli ekuhlunga esicongweni:

- **Lilanga Lekucala / Lilanga Lekugcina** — Ngekwehluleka kubuka konkhe kukhombisa emaviki langu-12 latako. Faka emalanga akho lokhetsile kute wandzise nobe unciphise lokukhonjiswa.
- **Umnyango** — Shintjela kulomunye umnyango ngaphandle kwekushiya kubuka konkhe.
- **Luhlobo Lweluhlelo** — Hlunga uye kuloluhlobo lolukhetsekile ngekhatsi kwemnyango lokhetsile.
- **Kungakagcwaliswa kuphela** — Vula loku kute ufihle emalayini lapho onkhe emalanga sekagcwalisiwe, kute ugcine kucondzana nesikhundla lesisadzinga umvolontiya.

Cindzetela **Filter** ngemuva kwekushintja kute ubuyeketa ithebula.

## Kukhipha Kuya ku-CSV

Cindzetela **Export CSV** kute ukhiphe ithebula lanyalo njengeshidi lekubala. Kukhipha kufaka tonkhe tikhundla kanye nesabelo sebavolontiya sefingqi lwemalanga lohlungile, kwenta kube lula kwabelana nebaholi bemnyango nobe kuphrinta emihlanganweni yekuhlela.

:::info
Kukhipha kwe-CSV kubonakalisa yonkhe kuhlunga lokusetjentiswe kwamanje — kuphela emalanga kanye noluhlobo lweluhlelo lokukhonjiswe kuthebula lokufakwa kulokukhishiwe.
:::
