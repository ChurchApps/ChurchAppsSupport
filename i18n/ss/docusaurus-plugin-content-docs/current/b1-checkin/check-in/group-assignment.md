# Kwabela Emacembu

<div class="article-intro">

Nawucindzetela inkinobho yesikhatsi senkhonzo eskrinini sekubuyeketa umndeni, i-app ivula skrini sekukhetsa licembu. Lapha ukhetsa kutsi ngutsini licembu nobe likilasi lelifanele lilunga lemndeni kutsi kuloko sikhatsi senkhonzo lelitsite.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngembi Kwekucala</h4>

- [Buyeketa umndeni wakho](./household-review) eskrinini sekubuyeketa umndeni
- Vula lilunga lofuna kulabela bese ucindzetela inkinobho yesikhatsi senkhonzo
- Cinisekisa kutsi umlawuli webandla lakho [usewakhile emacembu](../../b1-admin/groups/creating-groups.md) futsi awahlanganisile netikhatsi tenkhonzo ku-B1 Admin

</div>

## Emacembu Ahlelwe Njani

Emacembu akhonjiswa **etigabeni**. Sigaba ngasinye sivela njengekhadi lelikhombisa:

- **Ligama lesigaba** (sibonelo, "Tingane", "Bantsha", "Bantfu Labadzala")
- **Inani lemacembu** kulesigaba

Cindzetela likhadi lesigaba kute usivule bese ubona emacembu lawodwa langekhatsi. Sicindzetele futsi kute usisonte.

## Kukhetsa Licembu

1. Cindzetela **sigaba** kute usivule.
2. Buyeketa luhlu lwemacembu langekhatsi kwalesigaba. Licembu ngalinye likhombisa ligama lalo kanye neluphawu lwekukhetsa ngasesandleni sesekudla.
3. Cindzetela **ligama lelicembu** lofuna kulabela.

I-app iyagcina kukhetsa kwakho bese ikubuyisela [kuskrini sekubuyeketa umndeni](./household-review). Inkinobho yesikhatsi senkhonzo yalelo lunga itawukhombisa manje ligama lelicembu lokulikhetsile.

## Umyalo Wemaminyaka Netibanga

Nangabe libandla lakho selihlelile emaminyaka nobe tibanga temakamelo, luhlu luyakuhambisa endzaweni lefanele: emakamelo lomuntfu afanelekelako **ayacaciswa**, kantsi emakamelo langaphandle kweminyaka nobe sibanga sakhe **ayafiphala**. Likamelo lelifiphele lingakhonjwa nakunjalo, kodvwa umsebenti webandla kufanele acinisekise loko kukhetsa. Iminyaka netibanga tibalwa kusukela ekusukeni kwelusuku lwekukhushulwa kwesibanga sebandla lakho, ngako emakamelo ahlala aliqiniso ngesikhatsi sekuphenduka kwemnyaka wesikolwa.

## Kukhetsa "Kute"

Nangabe unquma kutsi lilunga lemndeni akufanele lilabelwe licembu kuloko sikhatsi senkhonzo, cindzetela inkinobho letsi **None** ngaphansi kweskrini. Loku kususa kwabelwa lokukhona kwelicembu kwaloko sikhatsi senkhonzo bese kukubuyisela kuskrini sekubuyeketa umndeni.

:::tip
Sebentisa kukhetsa **None** kubantfu labadzala labaya enkhonzweni lenkhulu labangadzingi kubhaliswa kulicembu nobe kulikilasi lelitsite.
:::

## Kushintja Kwabelwa Kwelicembu

Kute ushintje kwabelwa lokusewentiwe:

1. Buyela [eskrinini sekubuyeketa umndeni](./household-review).
2. Vula lilunga lofuna kushintja licembu lalo.
3. Cindzetela inkinobho yesikhatsi senkhonzo (lekhombisa manje ligama lelicembu lelabelwe kwamanje).
4. Khetsa lelinye licembu nobe ucindzetele **None**.

:::info
Emacembu netigaba tihlelwa ngumlawuli webandla lakho ku-B1 Admin. Nangabe licembu lingekho nobe likusigaba lelingasilo, cela umlawuli wakho kutsi abuyekete [kuhlelwa kwemacembu](../../b1-admin/groups/creating-groups.md).
:::

## Tinyatselo Letilandzelako

Ngemuva kwekwabela emacembu, buyela [eskrinini sekubuyeketa umndeni](./household-review) kute:

- Wabele emacembu kumalunga lamanye emndeni
- [Wengete sivakashi](./adding-guests) nangabe kukhona lophindzelako nemndeni wakho
- [Ucedze kubhalisa](./completing-checkin) nase konkhe kwabelwa kulungile
