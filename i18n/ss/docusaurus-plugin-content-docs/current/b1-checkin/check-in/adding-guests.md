# Kwengeta Tivakashi

<div class="article-intro">

Nangabe kukhona lophindzelako emndenini wakho kantsi kudzinga kubhalisa nemndeni wakho, ungamengeta njengesivakashi ngco esukela ku-kiosk. Loku kuyasita nangetingane letivakashile, tihlobo, nobangani labangakabi khona esimeni sebandla.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngembi Kwekucala</h4>

- [Funa umndeni wakho](./looking-up-members) bese ukhetsa lilunga kuletiphumo tekufuna
- Yiba [kuskrini sekubuyeketa umndeni](./household-review) lapho ungabona khona emalunga emndeni wakho

</div>

## Kwengeta Sivakashi Njani

1. Kusukela kuskrini sekubuyeketa umndeni, cindzetela inkinobho letsi **Engeta Sivakashi** lekhona ngaphansi kweluhlu lwemalunga emndeni.
2. Ifomu yekwengeta sivakashi iyavela inetinsimu letimbili:
   - **Ligama Lekucala** -- Faka ligama lekucala lesivakashi.
   - **Sibongo** -- Faka sibongo sesivakashi.
3. Cindzetela inkinobho letsi **Engeta Sivakashi** kute ugcine.

Sivakashi siyengetwa emndenini wakho bese ubuyiselwa kuskrini sekubuyeketa umndeni. Sivakashi lesisha sitawuvela eluhlwini lwakho lwemalunga kanye nalamanye emalunga emndeni wakho.

:::info
Nangabe ligama lesivakashi lifana nalomunye lomuntfu losele akhona kusistimu longesilo lilunga, i-app itawuhlanganisa naloo rekhodi lesele ikhona esikhundleni sekwakha lelinye lelifanako. Loku kusita kugcina i-database yebandla lakho ihlanzekile.
:::

## Ngemuva Kwekwengeta Sivakashi

Nangabe sivakashi sesivela eluhlwini lwemndeni wakho, ungakhona:

- **Vula likhadi lakhe** bese [uyamabela licembu](./group-assignment), njengaloo lamanye emalunga emndeni.
- **[Bhalisa](./completing-checkin)** umndeni wonkhe kufaka nesivakashi.

## Kukhansela

Nangabe ushintja umcabango wakho, cindzetela inkinobho letsi **Khansela** ngaphansi kwefomu yekwengeta sivakashi kute ubuyele kuskrini sekubuyeketa umndeni ngaphandle kwekwengeta muntfu.

:::tip
Kwengeta sivakashi ku-kiosk kudala irekhodi lemuntfu ku-database yebandla lakho. Umlawuli webandla lakho angabuye abuyekete imininingwane lephelele yesivakashi kamuva ku-B1 Admin ngaphansi kwe[Bantfu](../../b1-admin/people/adding-people.md).
:::

## Sinyatselo Lesilandzelako

Ngemuva kwekwengeta tivakashi kanye [nekwabela emacembu](./group-assignment) njengobe kudzingeka, usulungele [kucedza kubhalisa](./completing-checkin).
