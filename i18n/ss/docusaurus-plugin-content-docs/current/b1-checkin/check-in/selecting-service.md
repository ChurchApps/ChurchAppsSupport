# Kukhetsa Inkhonzo

<div class="article-intro">

Sinyatselo sekucala sekubhalisa konkhe kutsi ukhetse kutsi ngutsini inkhonzo lolawuya kuyo. Skrini setinkhonzo sivela kusenta ngemuva kwekutsi i-app icedze kulayisha bese sinquma kutsi ngutiphi tikhatsi tenkhonzo nemacembu latotfolakala kulendlela lesele yekubhalisa.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngembi Kwekucala</h4>

- [Ngena](../getting-started/logging-in) ku-B1 Church Checkin bese ukhetsa libandla lakho
- Cinisekisa kutsi umlawuli webandla lakho [usewahlele tinkhonzo netikhatsi tato](../../b1-admin/attendance/setup.md) ku-B1 Admin

</div>

## Kusebenta Njani

1. Ngemuva kwekungena (nobe ngemuva kwekungena ngekutentela kutsi uphindzele), i-app ikhombisa **skrini setinkhonzo**.
2. Utawubona luhlu lwato tonkhe tinkhonzo lesetihleliwe ebandleni lakho. Inkhonzo ngayinye ivela njengekhadi lelikhombisa ligama lenkhonzo (sibonelo, "Sonto Ekuseni" nobe "Lesibili Kusihlwa").
3. Cindzetela inkhonzo loya kuyo.

I-app iyalayisha tikhatsi tenkhonzo nemacembu lahlobene naloo nkhonzo, bese ikuyisa [kuskrini sekufuna lilunga](./looking-up-members).

:::info
Tinkhonzo tihlelwa ngumlawuli webandla lakho ku-B1 Admin ngaphansi kwesigaba sekubakhona. Nangabe ungayiboni inkhonzo loyilindzele, cela umlawuli wakho kutsi ahlole kutsi seyakhiwe [ekuhleleni kwekubakhona](../../b1-admin/attendance/setup.md).
:::

## Loko Lokwenteka Ngemuva Kwemakhona

Nawukhetsa inkhonzo, i-app itfola tintfo letintsatfu kusevha:

- **Tikhatsi tenkhonzo** taloo nkhonzo (sibonelo, inkhonzo yinye ingaba netikhatsi te-9:00 ekuseni ne-11:00 ekuseni).
- **Emacembu** latofakalako kusikhatsi ngasinye senkhonzo (sibonelo, iNesari, iPreschool, iElementary).
- **Kuhlanganiswa kwemacembu netikhatsi tenkhonzo** lokunquma kutsi ngutiphi emacembu latfolakala ngasiphi sikhatsi.

Lolwati lugcinwa lapha ku-tablet kute lonkhe luchungechunge lwekubhalisa lube lolusheshako futsi luphendvule ngekushesha.

:::tip
Nangabe libandla lakho linenkhonzo yinye kuphela lehleliwe, kusadzingeka uyicindzetele kute uchubeke. I-app ayikhetsi inkhonzo yinye yona ngekwayo.
:::

## Sinyatselo Lesilandzelako

Ngemuva kwekukhetsa inkhonzo, [utawufuna umndeni wakho](./looking-up-members) ngenombolo yelucingo nobe ngeligama.
