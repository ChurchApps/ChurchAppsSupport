# LiBhayibheli

<div class="article-intro">

B1 Mobile ifaka umfundzi weliBhayibheli lowakhelwe ngekhatsi kute ufundze imiBhalo Lengcwele ngco kusuka ku-app. Umfundzi ufakwe ngco esikrinini seliBhayibheli, ekunika kufinyelela ngekushesha kunome nguliphi liphindze ngaphandle kwekushintja kuya kulenye i-app.

</div>

- Libandla lakho kufanele libe nesisici seliBhayibheli sivuliwe

</div>

## Kufundza LiBhayibheli

1. Vula sikrini se-**LiBhayibheli** endzaweni yekuhamba-hamba.
2. Umfundzi uyalayisha ngeliphindze lelicalako.
3. Sebentisa tilawuli tekuhamba-hamba lekhatsi kwemfundzi kute uye kulenye incwadzi, sehlukwana, nobe vesi.

Umfundzi weliBhayibheli usebenta ngemandla e-YouVersion nobe lomunye umniniyo lolungiselelwe libandla lakho. Kufundza kusebenta ngendlela lefanako nome ngabe nguwuphi umniniyo losetjentiswako.

## Kuhamba-Hamba Emabhalweni

Umfundzi lofakiwe unika tilawuli tekuhamba-hamba emiBhalweni. Ungakwati:

- Kubuka uye encwadzini lecondzile yeliBhayibheli
- Kweqa uye esehlukwaneni lesitsite
- Kukrola emavesini ngekhatsi kwesehlukwana

Umfundzi ugcwalisa sikrini sonkhe, akunikeza sikhala lesenele kute ufundze kahle edivayisini lakho.

:::tip
Yenta sikrini seliBhayibheli sibe incenye yenchubo yakho yemihla ngemihla -- yindlela lesheshako yekufundza imiBhalo nomakuphi lapho ukhona. Uyihlanganise ne-[Vesi Lwelilanga](./verse-of-the-day) kutfola kukhutsatwa mihla yonkhe.
:::

## Kutfolakala

Sikrini seliBhayibheli sikhona nakuba libandla lakho livule lesisici. Uma ungasiboni sikhetso se-**LiBhayibheli** endzaweni yekuhamba-hamba, kungenteka mphatsi welibandla lakho angakasifaki ku-app. Tsindzana nemphatsi welibandla lakho kute ucele.
