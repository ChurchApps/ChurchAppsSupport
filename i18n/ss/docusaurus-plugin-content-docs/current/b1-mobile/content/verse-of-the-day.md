<div class="article-intro">

## Kubuka Vesi

1. Vula sikrini se-**Vesi Lwelilanga** endzaweni yekuhamba-hamba.
2. Sikrini sikhombisa vesi lwanamuhla njengesitfombe lesihlelwe.
3. Vesi lolusha luvela ngekutentekela lilanga nelilanga.

Akukho lokudzingekile kutsi ukwente -- vula nje sikrini bese vesi lwalelilanga luyakhonjiswa.

## Kukhonjiswa Lokutilungisako

Sitfombe sevesi siyatilungisa ngekutentekela kute sifanele indlela lidivayisi lakho lelibhekise ngayo. Kuya ngendlela lolubambe ngayo lucingo lwakho nobe itablet, utawubona linye lalamafomethi lamatsatfu:

- **Ema (9:16)** -- Lulungiselelwe emalucingweni labanjwe ngco phansi
- **Lucaca (16:9)** -- Ngcono kumatablet nobe emalucingweni labanjwe ngesihlangotsi
- **Sikwele (1:1)** -- Lisetjentiswa nakuba tilinganiso tesikrini sakho tiseduzane nekulingana

I-app iyabona indlela lidivayisi lakho lelibhekise ngayo bese ikhetsa lifomethi lelicono, kute vesi lihlale libukeka kahle nomangabe ulibuka njani. Uma uphendula idivayisi lakho, sitfombe siyabuyeketa kute sifanele indlela lensha.

:::tip
Yenta Vesi Lwelilanga libe incenye yenchubo yakho yasekuseni. Vula i-app lilanga nelilanga kutfola sikhatsi sekuticabangela ngaphambi kwekucala lilanga lakho.
:::

## Kutfolakala

Vesi Lwelilanga sisici lesakhelwe ngekhatsi se-B1 Mobile. Uma ungasiboni endzaweni yekuhamba-hamba, kungenteka mphatsi welibandla lakho angakasifaki ku-app. Tsindzana nemphatsi welibandla lakho kute ucele.
