# Likhasi Lasekhaya

<div class="article-intro">

Likhasi lasekhaya sisikrini sakho sasekhaya ku-B1 Mobile. Likunika kufinyelela lokusheshako etici letinikelwa libandla lakho, lihleleke ngendlela lawusebentisa ngayo i-app. Simo saso siyaguquka ngekuhamba kwesikhatsi kute sibeke tintfo lotisebentisa kakhulu embili.

</div>

## Ingcenye Yekwemukela

Etikwelikhasi lasekhaya, utawubona umlayeto wekwemukela kanye nelogo yelibandla lakho (nome libito lelibandla nangabe kute logo lesetjentiswako) kanye nemutsho lotsi "hlala uxhumene". Lengcenye iciniseka kutsi ngukuphi libandla loliyibonako njengamanje.

## Tixhumanisi Letikhonjiswako

Ngaphansi kwengcenye yekwemukela, sici losisebentisa kakhulu sivela njengelikhadi lelikhulu **(hero card)** lelinesitfombe sangemuva kanye nelibito lesici. Lesi sisici lowusisebentisa kakhulu, sikhushulelwe embili kute ukhone kusifinyelela ngekucindzetela kanye.

## Emakhadi Lakhonjiswako

Ngaphansi kwelikhadi lelikhulu, utawubona emakhadi lamabili **lakhonjiswako** lakhonjiswa eceleni kwalinye nalinye. Lawa amela tici tesibili nesitsatfu lotisebentisa kakhulu. Likhadi ngalinye likhombisa sitfombe sangemuva kanye nelibito lesici. Cindzetela nome ngukuphi kulamakhadi kute uye ngco kulesosici.

## Tento Letisheshako

Letinye tici letisele tivela ku-**Grid Yetento Letisheshako** ngaphansi kwemakhadi lakhonjiswako. Sici ngasinye sikhombisa luphawu (icon) kanye nelibeleta. Lawa ngutinye tonkhe tixhumanisi tekuhamba lelibandla lakho lelikuhlele, likupha kufinyelela kuko konkhe kusukela [emacembu](../groups/) ne[kunikela](../giving/) kuya [ekungeneni](../checkin/), [tinshumayelo](../content/sermons.md), nakunye lokunyenti.

## Indlela Simo Sekubukeka Lesiguqukako Ngayo

Simo sekubukeka kwelikhasi lasekhaya siyeva ngendlela lawusebentisa ngayo i-app. Njalo nawucindzetela sici, i-app iyagcina kutsi ulisebentisa kangakanani. Tici lotisebentisa kakhulu tiya embili kwelikhasi lasekhaya:

- Sici **losisebentisa kakhulu** sibayilikhadi lelikhulu.
- Tici **tesibili nesitsatfu lotisebentisa kakhulu** tibayemakhadi lakhonjiswako.
- **Konkhe lokunye** kuvela ku-Grid Yetento Letisheshako.

Nangabe ungakaze usebentise i-app, tici tivela ngendlela levamile lelibandla lakho leliyihlele. Ngekuhamba kwesikhatsi, likhasi lasekhaya liyaphindze lihlelwe kabusha kute lihambisane nemikhuba yakho.

:::info
Tici letitfolakala kulikhasi lakho lasekhaya tihlelwa ngumphatsi welibandla lakho nge-[B1 Admin](../../b1-admin/). Nangabe ungasiboni sici losilindzele, tsintsana nelihhovisi lelibandla lakho.
:::

## Kuvuselela Likhasi Lasekhaya

Dvonsa ngentansi kulikhasi lasekhaya kute ulivuselele. Loku kuphindze kulayishe tixhumanisi kanye nekucuketfwe kwelibandla lakho kute ucinisekise kutsi ubona imininingwane yakamuva.

## Kuzulazula

Kusukela elikhasini lasekhaya, ungakhona kufinyelela nakuyiphi ingcenye ye-app:

- Cindzetela **luphawu lwe-hamburger** (imicondvo lemitsatfu lehambisana) esikhungweni sangesekudla lengetulu kute uvule imenyu ye-drawer lenetintfo tonkhe tekuhamba.
- Cindzetela **luphawu lwensimbi** esikhungweni sangesekudla lengetulu kute ubone [tatiso takho](../community/notifications.md).
- Cindzetela nome ngukuphi likhadi nome sento lesisheshako kute uye ngco kulesosici.
