# Layini Yesikhatsi

<div class="article-intro">

**Layini Yesikhatsi** likhasi lekucala leliyimo yesekhutseki leliphothali yakho yemalungu ye-B1.church. Inika i-feed yemsebenti lekhombisa buchwepheshe bekugcina emacembini nasebandleni lakho, kugcina konkhe endzaweni yinye lelula.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekutsi Uce</h4>

- Kufanele ube [ungenile](../getting-started/logging-in.md) ku-akhawunti yakho ye-B1.church.

</div>

## Loko Lekukhonjiswa Ligi-Layini Yesikhatsi

Layini Yesikhatsi ikhombisa i-feed yetigcivi kanye netibuyekezo, letingahlanganisa:

- **Tingcoco Temacembu** -- Imilayeto kanye netingcoco tetive [temacembu](../groups/) longowawo.
- **Imisebenti** -- Imisebenti leyabelwe wena nome emacembu akho.
- **Imicimbi** -- Imicimbi letako lehlobene nawe nemacembu akho.
- **Tifundvo** -- Kharikhulamu ne[kwati kwetifundvo](../content/lessons.md) lokwabiwe nemacembu akho.
- **Tishumayelo** -- [Tishumayelo](../content/sermons.md) letisha letifakwe libandla lakho.

Sigcivi ngasinye ku-feed sikhombisa sihloko sesigcivi, licembu lesikilo (nangabe kukhona), kanye nekutsi kade nakanjani sifakwe.

## Kubuka Imininingwane Yesigcivi

Layini Yesikhatsi isebentisa endlalelo lene luhlu nemininingwane. I-feed yakho ihlelwe ngasesancele sekhasi. Chofota kunoma ngusiphi sigcivi kute uvule imininingwane yaso lephelele epanyeni yangasesokudla. Kusukela embukweni wemininingwane, ungafundza sicuko lesiphelele sesigcivi futsi usebentelane naso.

Chofota **Buyela Etibuyekezweni** etulu kwephaneli yemininingwane kute ubuyele eluhlwini lwe-feed.

## Kuhlala Uxhumene

Layini Yesikhatsi ihlanganisa imisebenti lesuka kuwo onkhe emacembu akho ibe yi-feed yinye, ngalendlela awudzingi kuhlola licembu ngalinye ngalinye. Kumkhuba lomuhle kuvakashela Layini Yesikhatsi yakho njalo kute uhlale wati ngaloko lokwentekako ebandleni lakho.

:::tip
Nangabe Layini Yesikhatsi yakho ingenalutfo, kungenteka awukamele emacembu. Vakashela likhasi le[Emacembu](../groups/browsing-groups.md) kute utfole emacembu longangenela kuwo, nome ucele mphatsi webandla lakho nome umholi welicembu kutsi akufake.
:::
