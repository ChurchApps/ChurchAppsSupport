# Luhlu Lwenkhonzo

<div class="article-intro">

Nawuvula uhlelo, incenye ye-**Luhlu Lwenkhonzo** ikhombisa kulandzelana kwetintfo taleyo nkhonzo. Loku kukupha wena netfimba lakho kubuka lokufananako kwekuhamba kwenkhonzo, kufaka ekhatsi sikhatsi, imininingwane yengoma, kanye netichazo taleyo naleyo ncenye.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Kufanele [ube ungenile](../getting-started/logging-in.md) ku-akhawundi yakho ye-B1.church.
- Vula uhlelo kusuka [kuluhlu lwetinhlelo](./viewing-plans.md) takho kuze ubone luhlu lwayo lwenkhonzo.

</div>

## Loko Lokukhonjiswa Luhlu Lwenkhonzo

Luhlu lwenkhonzo luboniswa njengeluhlu lwetintfo ngekulandzelana lokutawuvela ngako ngesikhatsi senkhonzo. Incenye ngayinye ihlanganisa:

- **Sikhatsi lesihlangene** -- Sikhatsi lesihamba kusukela ekucaleni kwenkhonzo, kuze wati kutsi incenye ngayinye ilindzeleke kucala nini.
- **Ligama lencenye kanye nenchazelo** -- Ligama lentfo (njengeligama lengoma, umtsandzazo, nobe simemetelo) kanye nato tonkhe timiselo nobe tichazo.
- **Sikhatsi** -- Kutsi kulindzeleke kutsatse sikhatsi lesingakanani leyo ncenye.

Tintfo tihlelwe ngaphansi kwe-**tihloko** letihlanganisa tintfo letihlobene (sibonelo, "Sethi Yekuhlabelela" nobe "Umlayeto"). Loku kwenta kube lula kubuka sakhiwo lesiphelele senkhonzo.

## Kucuketfwe Kwesifundvo

Nangabe uhlelo lunekucuketfwe kwesifundvo lokusuka kubaniki-nsita njenge-[Lessons.church](../content/lessons.md), utawubona kubuka kwaloko kucuketfwe ngekhatsi koluhlu lwenkhonzo. Loku kungahlanganisa tincenye tesifundvo, tinsita tekwetfulwa, kanye naletinye tinsita letidzingwa tfimba lakho enkhonzweni.

## Kushicilela Luhlu Lwenkhonzo

Ungashicilela luhlobo loluhleliwe lweluhlu lwenkhonzo kuze uluphatse ngelilanga lenkhonzo.

1. Vula uhlelo kusuka kuluhlu lwakho lwe-**Tinhlelo**.
2. Encenyeni ye-**Luhlu Lwenkhonzo**, chofota luphawu lweku-**shicilela** ekhoneni lelisetulu lasekudla.
3. Kuvula liwindi lelisha lelinesakhiwo lesingashicilelwa lelihlanganisa:
   - **Lusuku lwenkhonzo** etulu.
   - **Kwabelwa kwetfimba** kuhlelwe ngetigaba ngasekhoshi, kukhombisa sikhundla ngasinye kanye nekutsi ngubani losigcwalisako.
   - **Luhlu lwenkhonzo lolophelele** ngasekudla kanye netikholomu tesikhatsi, imininingwane yentfo, kanye nesikhatsi.
4. Ibhokisi yekushicilela yebrawuza yakho iyavuleka ngekutentakalelayo kuze ukhone kushicilela nobe ugcine njenge-PDF.

:::tip
Luhlu lwenkhonzo loloshicilelwe luyintfo lenhle kutsi ube nayo ngesikhatsi setirihisali nobe enkhonzweni ngalokwakhona. Luhlanganisa bunye luhlu lwetfimba kanye noluhlu lwenkhonzo lolophelele ekhasini elilodvwa.
:::
