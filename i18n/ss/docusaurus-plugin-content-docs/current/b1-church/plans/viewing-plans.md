# Kubuka Tinhlelo

<div class="article-intro">

Likhasi le-Tinhlelo likupha kubuka lokucacile kwato tonkhe tinhlelo tenkhonzo lowabelwe kuto. Lisebentisa luhlobo lwe-master-detail kuze ukhone kuphequlula tinhlelo takho ngekushesha bese ungena ngekhatsi kwimininingwane yanoma ngutiphi.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Kufanele [ube ungenile](../getting-started/logging-in.md) ku-akhawundi yakho ye-B1.church.
- Kufanele wabelwe kutsatsa incenye kulokungenani luhlelo lulodvwa lwenkhonzo. Nangabe awuboni tinhlelo, tsindzabana nemphatsi welibandla lakini nobe umholi webufundisi.

</div>

## Kufinyelela Tinhlelo Takho

1. Chofota **Tinhlelo** ku-[bha yekuhamba yeceleni](../getting-started/navigating.md).
2. Utawubona luhlu lwetinhlelo takho ehlangotsini lasenshonalanga lelikhasi.

## Luhlu Lwetinhlelo

Tinhlelo takho tibalisiwe ephanela laseshonalanga ngaphansi kwe-**Tinhlelo Tami**. Sibhalo ngasinye sikhombisa:

- **Ligama loluhlelo** (sibonelo, "Inkhonzo Yangesonto" nobe "Bufundisi Bebantfwana")
- **Lusuku lwenkhonzo**
- **Sikhundla** sakho (indzima losigcwalisako)
- **Simo** sakho -- lesibonisiwe ngemibala kuze ubone ngekushesha kutsi ngabe uvumile, walile, nobe awukaphendvuli

Etulu koluhlu, utawubona nalelinye likhetfo leliteka **Emalanga Lengekho**. Chofota kulo kuze uphatse emalanga longeko yekukhonta. Ungayengeta, uhlele, futsi ususe tinhlobo temalanga lengekho, kuze baholi betfimba lakho bati kutsi bahlela njani ngawe.

:::info
Libandla lakini lingatfumela **tikhumbuto** ngembi kwetinkhonzo lowabelwe kuto, nge-imeyili nanjengesexwayiso sohlelo. Nangabe awukaphenduli, ungakhona **Kuvuma** nobe **Kwala** ngalokucondzile kusuka kuncwadzi lwesikhumbuto -- akudzingekile kungena.
:::

## Kubuka Imininingwane Yohlelo

Chofota nawuphi uhlelo eluhlwini kuze uvule imininingwane yalo ephanela lelisenshonalanga. Kubuka kwemininingwane kuhlanganisa:

- **Imininingwane yesikhundla** -- Sikhundla sakho lowabelwe kuso kanye netikhatsi letidzingwako kanye nesimo sakho samanje. Nangabe awukaphendvuli, ungachofota **Vuma** nobe **Wala** ngalokucondzile kusuka kulokubuka.
- **Timiselo** -- Nome ngutiphi timiselo umenti woluhlelo latengeta kutfimba.
- **Luhlu Lwenkhonzo** -- Luhlu lwenkhonzo lolophelele kanye nesikhatsi, tintfo, kanye netichazo. Buka [Luhlu Lwenkhonzo](./service-order.md) kuze utfole imininingwane leyengetiwe.
- **Kwabelwa kwetfimba** -- Sifinyeto sabo bonkhe emalunga etfimba netikhundla tabo, kuze ubone kutsi ngubani lomunye lokhontako nawe.

:::tip
Nangabe unekwabelwa lokuningi kuto tonkhe tinhlelo letehlukene, luhlu luhleliwe ngelusuku kuze tibopho takho letitako tivele kucala. Buyela njalo kuze uhlale wati kutsi kwenteka kuphi luhlelo lwakho.
:::

## Kubuka Kwe-Titjhela Etifundvweni

Nangabe uhlelo lwakho lunekucuketfwe kwesifundvo lokusuka kubaniki-nsita (njenge-Lessons.church), utawubona inkhinobho yekushintjashintja ethebhini ye-**Luhlu Lwenkhonzo** lekuvumela kushintjela emkhatsini wekubuka kwe-**Sifinyeto** kanye ne-**Kufundzisa**.

- **Kubuka kwesifinyeto** -- Kukhombisa luhlu lwenkhonzo kanye nesikhatsi netincenye letiyisisekelo tesifundvo, lokulungele kubuyeketa ngekushesha kutsi kutawenteka njani nanini
- **Kubuka kwekufundzisa** -- Kuvula sethulo sesifundvo lesigcwele likhasi, lelakhelwe titjhela, linemiyalo lehleliwe, imidiya lefakwe ngekhatsi, timiselo, kanye nekucondzisa ngalinyatselo nelinye

Kubuka kwekufundzisa kuhlela kucuketfwe kwesifundvo tincenye letingahamba, letiboniswa njengemathebhu etulu. Ungakhona:

- Chofota ithebhu yencenye kuze weke ngalokucondzile kuleyo ncenye yesifundvo
- Skrola ngekhatsi kwekucuketfwe kwesifundvo, futsi ithebhu lesebentako iyatisebuyisela ngekutentakalelayo kuze ifanane nesikhundla sakho
- Chofota tintfo temidiya (emavidiyo, titfombe, imisebenti) kuze utibuke ku-bhokisi lengenele
- Bona tinhlobo temisebenti tibonisiwe ngendlela lehlukile: **Sho** ivela emabhabhulini ekukhuluma, **Yenta** ngembhalo losindzako, kanye ne-**Phawula** kubonisiwe ngemibala legqamile

:::tip
Kubuka kwekufundzisa kusita kakhulu nawuhola likilasi nobe licembu lelincane, njengoba kwetfula konkhe kucuketfwe kwesifundvo sakho ngendlela lecocekile, lengenaphazamiso, lelungiselwe kusebentisa ku-mobile nakuma-tablet.
:::
