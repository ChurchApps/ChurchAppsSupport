# Umlandvo Wemnikelo

<div class="article-intro">

Nawungenile, ungabona rekhodi leliphelele lemnikelo wakho wangaphambili futsi ukhicite tincwadzi tekunikela tetintela. Umlandvo wakho wemnikelo utfolakala engcenyeni ye-**Donate** ye-akhawunti yakho ye-B1.church.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Kudzingeka kutsi [ube ungenile](../getting-started/logging-in.md) ku-akhawunti yakho ye-B1.church.
- Kudzingeka kutsi ube wentile umnikelo linye noma ngesikhatsi. Bona [Kwenta Umnikelo](./making-donations.md) kucala.

</div>

## Kubona Umlandvo Wakho

1. Ngena ku-akhawunti yakho ye-B1.church.
2. Yaka engcenyeni ye-**Donate**.
3. Chofoza **History** ephanelini lesencele kuze ubone imnikelo yakho yangaphambili.

Umlandvo wakho wemnikelo ukhonjiswa njengethebula lenaletikolomu letilandzelako:

- **Date** -- Nini lapho umnikelo wentiwa khona.
- **Method** -- Kutsi wakhokha njani (sibonelo, "Card - Visa" nome "Bank - ACH").
- **Fund** -- Kutsi umnikelo ucondziswe kuwuphi sikhwama (sibonelo, iSikhwama Lesijwayelekile, iSikhwama Sekwakha).
- **Amount** -- Inani lemali yemnikelo.

:::info
Imnikelo lesengekacutjwa itawuvela ilibhala lelitsi "Pending" ecaleni kwelibito lesikhwama, kanye nenani likhonjiswe ngembala lohlukile.
:::

## Kukhipha Netincwadzi Tekunyatselisa

Chofoza inkhinobho ye-**download** ehedeni lomlandvo Wemnikelo kuze ufinyelele tikhetho tekukhipha:

- **Current Year (CSV)** -- Layisha ifayela le-CSV lemnikelo wakho wonkhe wanyaka wamanje. Ungayivula lena kuhlelo lwesipredishithi.
- **Current Year (Print)** -- Vula incwadzi leyekunyatselisa yekunikela lengaba nyatselwa yanyaka wamanje.
- **Last Year (CSV)** -- Layisha ifayela le-CSV lemnikelo wakho wonkhe wanyaka lodlulile.
- **Last Year (Print)** -- Vula incwadzi leyekunyatselisa yekunikela lengaba nyatselwa yanyaka lodlulile.

:::tip
Nyatselisa incwadzi yakho yenyaka lodlulile yekunikela ekucaleni kwenyaka lomusha kuze ube nayo ilungile ngesikhatsi sentela. Incwadzi leyekunyatselisa ifaka yonkhe imininingwane loyidzingako kumarekhodi akho.
:::

## Kunikela Lokuphindzaphindzako

Kusuka engcenyeni ye-**Donate**, ungachofoza nakengcenyeni ye-**Recurring Donations** kuze uphatse nome ngukuphi kunikela lokuhleliwe losekukuhlelile. Le ngcenye ikuvumela kutsi ubone, ulungise, nome ukhansele kunikela lokuphindzaphindzako futsi uphatse tindlela takho tekukhokha letigciniwe.

Nangabe kunikela lokuphindzaphindzako kukhokhiswa nge-**Stripe**, ungakwati na**kuyimisa** esikhundleni sekuyikhansela -- chofoza i-icon yekumisa ecaleni kwemnikelo bese ucinisekisa. Kunikela kuyayima kuze uphindze uchofote i-icon lefanako kuze **uphindze uchale**. Umnikelo lomisiwe uba **Paused** eluhlwini. Kunikela lokuphindzaphindzako ngetinye tindlela tekukhokha kungalungiswa nome kukhanjelwe, kepha akukho kwati kumiswa.
