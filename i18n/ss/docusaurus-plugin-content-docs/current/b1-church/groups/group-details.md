# Imininingwane Yelicembu

<div class="article-intro">

Nawuchofota licembu kusuka ekhasini lemacembu, uyisiwa ekhasini lelinemininingwane yelicembu. Loko lokubonako kuya ngekutsi ungenile yini nobe cha, kanti emalunga langenile anelilungelo lekufinyelela kumathebhu nemasici lengetiwe.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Tfola licembu lofuna kulibuka nge [kubukela emacembu](./browsing-groups.md).
- Kuze utfole kufinyelela lokuphelele kuwo onkhe emathebhu, [ngena](../getting-started/logging-in.md) ku-akhawundi yakho.

</div>

## Kubuka Kwesihambeli (Longakangeni)

Nangabe ungakangeni, utawubona loku lokulandzelako:

- **Incenye lenkhulu** -- Indzawo lenkhulu leboniswa sitfombe selicembu, ligama, sikhatsi sekuhlangana, kanye nendzawo yekuhlangana.
- **Inchazelo** -- Inchazelo lephelele yelicembu lechaza injongo yalo kanye naloko lokulindzelekile.
- **Baholi** -- Titfombe kanye nemagama ebaholi belicembu. Ungachofota ligama lemholi kuze ubuke iphrofayela yakhe.
- **Imicimbi yekhalendari** -- Kuvela imicimbi lengeka-3 letako yelicembu, ngayinye ikhombisa lusuku kanye neligama lemcimbi. Nangabe licembu lite imicimbi letako, lencenye iyafihlwa.
- **Ifomu yekutsindzabana** -- Ifomu lekuvumela kutfumela umlayeto ngalokucondzile kubaholi belicembu. Faka **ligama lakho lekucala**, **sibongo**, **imeyili**, **inombolo yelucingo**, kanye ne**mlayeto**, bese uchofota **Tfumela**. Nangabe kunebaholi labaningi, ungakhetsa lokhonye kubaholi labatsatswayo kusuka kuluhlu.

## Kubuka Kwelilunga (Longenile)

Nawungenile, likhasi lelinemininingwane yelicembu likhombisa incenye lenkhulu kanye nemathebhu langeceleni. Emathebhu latfolakalako:

- **Imininingwane Yelicembu** -- Inchazelo lephelele yelicembu, leboniswa ngesakhiwo lesicebile.
- **Emalunga** -- Luhlu lwawo onkhe emalunga elicenjini, akhombisa sitfombe kanye neligama lawo. Ungachofota ligama lelilunga kuze uvakashele iphrofayela yalo ku-[luhlu lwemalunga](../community/member-directory.md).
- **Khalendari** -- Ikhalendari yelicembu leboniswa imihlangano kanye nemicimbi. Ungaphequlula imicimbi letako kanye nalendlulile.
- **Tingcoco** -- Indzawo lapho emalunga elicembu angatfumela khona imilayeto futsi asebentisane khona. Buka [Tingcoco Telicembu](./conversations.md) kuze utfole imininingwane leyengetiwe.
- **Tinsita** -- Emafayela kanye nemalinki lesabelwe licembu. Ungakhwishisa emafayela lalayishiwe nobe uvule tinsita letinelinki.
- **Kuya Ekhatsi** -- Kubonakala kubaholi belicembu kuphela. Kuvumela kulandzelela kutsi ngubani lobebekhona emihlanganweni yelicembu.

## Emakhono Emholi

Nangabe ungumholi welicembu, unemakhono lengetiwe ekuhleleni:

- **Hlela imininingwane yelicembu** -- Buyeketa ligama lelicembu, inchazelo, sikhatsi sekuhlangana, kanye netinye tinhlelo ngalokucondzile kusuka kuthebhu ye-**Imininingwane**.
- **Phatsa emalunga** -- Faka emalunga lamasha elicenjini nobe ususe lawakhona kusuka kuthebhu ye-**Emalunga**.
- **Yakha futsi uhlele imicimbi** -- Yengeta imicimbi ekhalendeni yelicembu. Nawakha umcimbi, ungabhukha emakamelo kanye netinsita kanye nawo — khetsa emakamelweni nasetinsiteni letitfolakalako, ubeke sikhatsi sekulungisa nekususa (nangabe kudzingeka), bese sicelo sekubhukha sitfunyelwa ngekutakhoma kuze sivunywe.
- **Phatsa tinsita** -- Layisha emafayela (kufika ku-100 MB yendzawo yekugcina), yengeta emalinki, nobe susa tinsita letikhona.
- **Tinsita Tebaholi** -- Finyelela kuthebhu lehlukene ye-**Tinsita (Baholi)** lenemafayela nemalinki lokuboniswa baholi belicembu kuphela.
- **Landzelela kuya ekhatsi** -- Bhala phansi kuya ekhatsi kwemihlangano yelicembu kusuka kuthebhu ye-**Kuya Ekhatsi**.

## Kungenela Licembu

Nawungenile futsi ungakabi lilunga, kuvela inkhinobho yekungenela ekhasini lelinemininingwane yelicembu. Ligama lenkhinobho lisekelwe emtsetfweni wekungena welicembu:

- **Ngenela Licembu** -- Licembu livulekile. Kuchofota loku kukungenisa ngalokushesha njengelilunga.
- **Cela Kungenela** -- Licembu lidzinga imvumo. Kuchofota loku kuvula ibhokisi lapho ungafaka khona umlayeto longakafuneki kumholi welicembu, bese utfumela sicelo sakho. Buka [Kucela Kungenela Licembu](./join-requests.md) kuze utfole imininingwane yekuphatsa ticelo takho.

Nangabe kute inkhinobho lebonakalako, licembu livalekile futsi emalunga kufanele afakwe ngesandla ngumholi nobe umphatsi.

:::info
Nangabe licembu livalekile futsi ufuna kungenela, sebentisa ifomu yekutsindzabana kubukeni kwesihambeli kuze utsindzabane nebaholi belicembu.
:::
