# Umbono weThishela wetiFundvo

<div class="article-intro">
Umbono weThishela unikeza sikhombakalo lesandzisiwe, se-skrini lophelele sekubuka sicuketfwe sesifundvo sinetincenye letihleliwe, lokwenta kube lula kuthishela nebaholi kutsi bazulazule etinsiteni tesifundvo ngesikhatsi sekilasi.
</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Ibandla lakho kufanele libe netifundvo letihleliwe eluhlelweni. Bona umhlahlandlela we-B1 Admin nge-[Kuhlela Tifundvo](../../b1-admin/serving/scheduling-lessons.md) kute utfole imininingwane.
- Udzinga kwabelwa kuloluhlelo kumbe ube nemvume yekulubona esayithini yakho ye-B1.church.
</div>

## Kuvula Umbono weThishela

Kute ufinyelele kumbono weThishela losandzisiwe wesifundvo:

1. Ya encenyeni ye-**Tinhlelo** ku-B1.church
2. Vula luhlelo lolunesifundvo lofuna kusifundzisa
3. Tfola sifundvo etintfweni teluhlelo
4. Chofoza sifundvo kute ubone imininingwane yaso
5. Chofoza inkinobho ye-**Umbono weThishela** kumbe **Umbono Losandzisiwe**

Sifundvo sitawuvuleka kusikhombakalo se-skrini lophelele lesakhelwe kufundzisa.

:::tip
Umbono weThishela usita kakhulu nawuphrojekthisa sicuketfwe sesifundvo eskrinini kumbe ku-TV kute ikilasi ibone, kumbe nawufundzisa usuka ku-tablet.
:::

## Tici Tembono weThishela

### Kuzulazula Ngemathebhu

Umbono weThishela uhlela sicuketfwe sesifundvo etincenyeni usebentisa emathebhu langetulu:

- Incenye ngayinye lenkhulu yesifundvo ivela njengelithebhu lelehlukene
- Chofoza noma yimuphi lithebhu kute ugudle uye kuleyoncenye ngqo
- Incenye lesebentako ikhonjiswa ngekukhanya kubar yemathebhu
- Emathebhu enta kube lula kugudla uye etincenyeni letehlukene tesifundvo

### Sicuketfwe Lesihushuzekako

Indzawo lenkhulu yesicuketfwe ikhombisa tonkhe tinsita tesifundvo:

- Hushuzela ngekusifundvo ngendlela lesemtsetfweni
- Uma uhushuza, lithebhu lelisebentako litenta lube ngumshini lifananisa nencenye loyibukako
- Onkhe umbhalo lohlelwe, titfombe, netiyalo tikhonjiswa ngekucacile

### Kudlala Kwemidiya

Nawe tifundvo tinemavidiyo, titfombe, kumbe lenye imidiya:

- Chofoza noma iyiphi intfo yemidiya kute uyivule ku-popup player
- Emavidiyo adlala ku-player levidiyo lekhetsekile lenetilawuli letijwayelekile
- Titfombe tivuleka ku-lightbox kute tibukwe ngesikrini lesigcwele
- Vala i-player yemidiya kute ubuyele esicuketfweni sesifundvo

### Tinsita Letingakhishelwa

Nangabe sifundvo sinemafayela langakhishelwa (emaphepha ekwenta, emaphepha ekupendza, njll.):

- Tinkinobho tekukhipha tiyavela intfo ngayinye
- Chofoza kute ukhiphele emafayela ngco kudivayisi lwakho
- Emafayela angahlanganisa ema-PDF, titfombe, kumbe letinye tinsita letinikelwe ngumakhi wesicuketfwe

## Kusebentisa Umbono weThishela Ekilasini

### Ngaphambi Kwekilasi

1. Vula sifundvo ku-Umbono weThishela ngaphambi kwekutsi ikilasi icale
2. Buyeketa tonkhe tincenye futsi utijwayeze sicuketfwe
3. Khipha noma ngutiphi tinsita letingaphrintwa lotidzingako
4. Hlola emavidiyo kumbe imidiya kute ucinisekise kutsi ayadlala kahle

### Ngesikhatsi Sekilasi

1. Gcina Umbono weThishela uvulekile kudivayisi lwakho kumbe uwuphrojekthise eskrinini
2. Sebentisa emathebhu kute ugudle emkhatsini wetincenye njengobe uchubeka nesifundvo
3. Dlala emavidiyo kumbe ukhombise titfombe ngesikhatsi lesifanele
4. Hushuzela etiyalweni njengobe kudzingekile ngaphandle kwekulahlekelwa yindzawo yakho

### Kuphrojekthiswa Eskrinini

Nawuphrojekthisa kwikilasi:

- Sakhiwo se-skrini lophelele sisusa tintfo letiphambukisako
- Umbhalo lomkhulu, lofundzeka kahle wenta sicuketfwe sibonakale kusuka kuwo onkhe emakhoneni egumbi
- Chofoza tintfo temidiya kute ukhombise emavidiyo kumbe titfombe kuyo yonkhe ikilasi
- Zulazula usebentisa imawusi lengenantsambo kumbe i-trackpad

## Kuvala Umbono weThishela

Kute uphume kumbono weThishela:

- Chofoza inkinobho ye-**Vala** (X) ekhoneni langenhla
- Kumbe cindzetela inkhinobho ye-**Esc** kukhibhodi yakho
- Utawubuyela kumbono wemininingwane yeluhlelo

## Kusebentisa ku-Mobile ne-Tablet

Umbono weThishela usebenta kuma-tablet nakuma-mobile:

- **Ema-tablet** -- Alungele kakhulu tithishela letifuna umhlahlandlela wesifundvo longahamba naye
- **Emafoni** -- Nawo ayasekelwa, nomakunjalo skrini lesincane singaba nendlela lengakalungeli kangako yekufundzisa
- **Indlela ye-landscape** -- Inikeza livo lekubukela lelingcono kakhulu kumadivayisi angumakhalekhukhwini

## Umehluko Kusuka Kumbono Lojwayelekile Wesifundvo

| Sici | Umbono Lojwayelekile | Umbono weThishela |
|---------|--------------|--------------|
| **Sakhiwo** | Sincane, sihambisana neluhlelo | Skrini lophelele, umbono lokhetsekile |
| **Kuzulazula** | Kuhushuza kuphela | Emathebhu + kuhushuza ngekukhanyisa ngekutitenzela |
| **Umdiya** | Ufakwe ekhasini | I-Player ye-popup yekubukela ngalokugcile |
| **Sikhala seskrini** | Ikhombisa letinye tintfo teluhlelo | Yandziswe kusicuketfwe sesifundvo |
| **Kulungele kakhulu** | Kuzulazula nekuhlela | Kufundzisa nekwetfula |

## Tindlela Letinhle Kakhulu

- **Lungiselela kusengaphambili** -- Buyeketa tifundvo ku-Umbono weThishela ngaphambi kwekilasi kute utijwayeze sakhiwo
- **Phawula tincenye** -- Bhala kutsi ngumaphi emathebhu lanetintfo tekwenta kumbe tekucocisana letibalulekile
- **Hlola imidiya** -- Cinisekisa kutsi emavidiyo netitfombe tiyavuleka kahle ngaphambi kwesikhatsi sekilasi
- **Khipha tinsita kusengaphambili** -- Ungalindzeli sikhatsi sekilasi kute ukhiphele tintfo letingaphrintwa
- **Sebentisa skrini lesikhulu** -- Phrojekthisa kumbe usebentise i-tablet kute utfole umuva lomuhle wekufundzisa

## Kulungisa Tinkinga

### Sifundvo asikavuli ku-Umbono weThishela

- Cinisekisa kutsi sifundvo besihleliwe kahle eluhlelweni
- Hlola kutsi unemvume yekubona luhlelo
- Vuselela likhasi bese uzama futsi

### Emavidiyo awadlali

- Hlola luxhumano lwakho lwe-inthanethi
- Cinisekisa kutsi idivayisi lwakho luvumela kudlalwa kwemidiya
- Zama kuvula ividiyo kwithebhu lelehlukene
- Labanye labaniketi besicuketfwe bangaba netivimbelo ekudlaleni kwevidiyo

### Sicuketfwe sibonakala sinqunyiwe ku-mobile

- Phendula idivayisi lwakho lube yindlela ye-landscape
- Ncipha kukhulu kancane nangabe kudzingekile
- Cabangela kusebentisa i-tablet kumbe idivayisi lenkhulu kuze kubonakale kangcono
