# Tintshumayelo

<div class="article-intro">

Likhasi leTintshumayelo likuvumela kutsi uzulazule futsi ubuke tintshumayelo letichajiwe tebandla lakho. Umtsamo wetintshumayelo webandla lakho ukhonjiswa usebentisa kubukeka nendlela lehleliwe leibandla lakho selikuhlelile, ngako kubukeka kutawufana nemake yebandla lakho.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Akudzingeki kungena kute ubone tintshumayelo. Likhasi leTintshumayelo lifinyeleleka nguwonkhe umuntfu.
- Ibandla lakho kufanele libe selingetile sicuketfwe setintshumayelo ngeliphothali laso lekuphatsa.

</div>

## Kuzulazula Tintshumayelo

1. Ya elikhasini le-**Tintshumayelo** kusuka eluhlwini lwekuzulazula lwesayithi yebandla lakho.
2. Utawubona luhlu lwetintshumayelo letitfolakalako, ngavamise tihlelwe ngeluhlu lwekudlala kumbe luchungechunge.
3. Chofoza kunoma yimuphi intshumayelo kute uyibuke kumbe uyilalele.

Tintshumayelo tingahlanganisa sicuketfwe sevidiyo kumbe sekulalelwa lesifakwe etinkhundleni letifana ne-YouTube, Vimeo, kumbe Facebook. Nawuchofoza intshumayelo, umdiya udlala ngco elikhasini.

## Loko Lotawukubona

Yinye ngayinye yentshumayelo ingakhombisa:

- **Sihloko** -- Ligama lentshumayelo
- **Umshumayeli** -- Ngubani lonikete umlayeto
- **Lusuku** -- Nini lapho intshumayelo yachajwa kumbe yashicelelwa khona
- **Sichasiso** -- Sifingqo sesicuketfwe sentshumayelo
- **Umfanekiso** -- Sitfombe sekubuka kusengaphambili sentshumayelo

Ibandla lakho lihlela tintshumayelo etinhlwini tekudlala kumbe kuluchungechunge. Loku kwenta kube lula kulandzela luchungechunge lwemaviki lamanyenti kumbe kutfola tintshumayelo mayelana nesihloko lesitsite.

:::tip
Nangabe ufuna kusakata bukhoma kunentshumayelo lechajiwe, hlola likhasi le-[Kusakata Bukhoma](./live-streaming.md) esikhundleni saloko.
:::

## Kutfolakala

Likhasi leTintshumayelo litfolakala nangabe ibandla lakho selingetile sicuketfwe setintshumayelo ngeliphothali laso lekuphatsa. Nangabe ungalitfoli lixhumana le-**Tintshumayelo** eluhlwini lwekuzulazula lwebandla lakho, kungenteka ibandla lakho lingakakahleli lesi sici. Tsindzana nemphatsi webandla lakho kutfola imininingwane leyengetiwe.
