# LiBhayibheli

<div class="article-intro">

I-B1.church ihlanganisa ne-Bible reader lesakhiwe ngekhatsi kuze ukhone kufundza umBhalo ngaphandle kwekushiya sayithi. Lomshini wekufundza ufakwe ngco ekhasini, ukunika kufinyelela lokusheshako kunoma ngukuphi umBhalo eceleni kwato letinye tinsita tebandla lakho.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Akudzingeki kungena (login) kuze usebentise i-Bible reader. Likhasi leliBhayibheli liyatfolakala emphakatsini wonkhe.
- Libandla lakho kufanele libe selifake likhasi leliBhayibheli ku-navigation yesayithi salo.

</div>

## Kufundza LiBhayibheli

1. Yendlulela ekhasini le-**Bible** kusuka ku-navigation yesayithi lebandla lakho.
2. Lomshini wekufundza uvula ku-Genesise 1:1 ngekwehluleka (default).
3. Sebentisa tilawuli te-navigation letiphakathi kwemshini wekufundza kute ushintje kuya kulencwadzi, sehluko, nome vesi lelihlukile.

I-Bible reader isebenta nge-**YouVersion** futsi ikunika kufinyelela eluhlwini lolubanti lwato tinguqulo teliBhayibheli etilwimini letinyenti.

## Kuhamba emiBhalweni

Lomshini wekufundza lofakiwe unika tilawuli tekuhamba emBhalweni. Ungakwati:

- Kubhrawuza uye encwadzini lecondzile yeliBhayibheli
- Kweqa uye esehlukweni lesitsite
- Kuskrola emavesini ngekhatsi kwesehluko
- Kushintja kuya kuenye inguqulo yeliBhayibheli usebentisa sikhetsi senguqulo

Lomshini wekufundza ugcwalisa indzawo yekwakheka yelikhasi, ukunika sikhala lesenele kuze ufundze ngekukhululeka.

:::tip
Incwadzi, sehluko, nenguqulo lokhetsile kuyakhunjulwa kuze lomshini wekufundza uvule kabusha lapho lowuyekile khona.
:::

:::tip
Ungabhukhimaka likhasi leliBhayibheli ku-brawuza yakho kuze ubuyele kulo ngekusheshisa ekufundzeni kwakho kwemihla ngemihla. Lihlanganise ne-[Verse of the Day](./verse-of-the-day.md) kute utfole sikhutsalo semBhalo mihla yonkhe.
:::

## Kutfolakala

Likhasi leliBhayibheli sisici lesakhiwe ngekhatsi se-B1.church. Nangabe ungaliboni linki le-**Bible** ku-navigation yesayithi lebandla lakho, kungenteka umphatsi webandla lakho akakalifaki ku-menu yesayithi. Tsintsana nemphatsi webandla lakho kutsi umcele alifake.
