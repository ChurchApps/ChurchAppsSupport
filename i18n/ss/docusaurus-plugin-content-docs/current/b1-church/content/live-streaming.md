# Kusakata Bukhoma

<div class="article-intro">

Likhasi le-Live Stream likuvumela kubuka tinkonzo tebandla lakho tisenteka, ngco ku-B1.church. Nawe ibandla lakho lisakata, ungangenela futsi ubambe cinini usebentisa tici letiphatselana ecaleni kwevidiyo.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Akudzingeki kungena kute ubuke kusakata bukhoma. Likhasi le-Stream lifinyeleleka nguwonkhe umuntfu.
- Ibandla lakho kufanele libe nesikhatsi sekusakata lesihlelwe. Hlola tikhatsi tenkonzo tebandla lakho kute wati kutsi kusakata bukhoma kutfolakala nini.

</div>

## Kubuka Kusakata Bukhoma

1. Ya elikhasini le-**Stream** kusuka eluhlwini lwekuzulazula lwesayithi yebandla lakho.
2. Nangabe inkonzo isenteka noma isitawucala, simeleli sevidiyo sitawutfwela ngokutenzela.
3. Buka kusakata ngco elikhasini.

Nangabe kute kusakata lokusenteka ngalesosikhatsi, ungase ubone sicuketfwe lesingaphandle kweinthanethi kumbe imininingwane mayelana nesikhatsi senkonzo lelandzelako lesihleliwe.

## Tici Letiphatselana

Likhasi lekusakata bukhoma lingaba nemathebhu laphatselanako ecaleni kwevidiyo, kuya ngaloko ibandla lakho likuhlelile. Loku kungahlanganisa:

- **Kucoca** -- Tfumela imilayeto futsi uphatselane nalabanye lababukako ngesikhatsi sekusakata. Kucoca kuvamise kuvulwa emiminithini lembalwa ngaphambi kwekutsi inkonzo icale futsi kuchubeke kutfolakala sikhashana ngemuva kwekutsi iphele. Ungakwati kucoca ngaphandle kwekungena -- ncumisela nje ligama lelitawubonakala nawucelwa.
- **Emathebhu langetiwe** -- Ibandla lakho lingase lengete emathebhu enteliwe latsi nemaxhumana kuma-bulletin, emanothi entshumayelo, [emakhasi ekupha](../giving/making-donations.md), kumbe letinye tinsita.

Chofoza etifanekisweni temathebhu kumbe emagameni kute ushintje emkhatsini wetici letitfolakalako ngesikhatsi ubuka.

## Ngaphambi Nangemuva Kwenkonzo

Ibandla lakho lihlela tikhatsi letitsite tekutsi nini lapho kusakata netici letiphatselanako titfolakala khona. Ungakwati kungenela kucoca ngaphambi kwekutsi inkonzo icale ngalokusemtsetfweni, lokukunika sikhatsi sekubingelela labanye. Ngemuva kwekutsi inkonzo iphele, kucoca kungahlala kuvuliwe sikhashana kute kuchubeke kukhulunywa.

:::info
Nangabe likhasi lekusakata likhombisa sicuketfwe lesingaphandle kweinthanethi kumbe sibalo sesikhatsi, inkonzo lelandzelako lelihleliwe ayikakali. Buyela uhlole ngesikhatsi lesibaliwe senkonzo.
:::

:::tip
Ufuna intshumayelo leyachajiwe loyiphuce? Vakashela likhasi le-[Tintshumayelo](./sermons.md) kute uzulazule imilayeto yakadzeni yebandla lakho.
:::

## Kutfolakala

Likhasi le-Live Stream litfolakala nangabe ibandla lakho selihlele sikhatsi sekusakata. Nangabe ungalitfoli lixhumana le-**Stream** eluhlwini lwakho lwekuzulazula, kungenteka ibandla lakho alikakuhleli kusakata bukhoma. Tsindzana nemphatsi webandla lakho kutfola imininingwane.
