# Kucuketfwe

<div class="article-intro">

I-B1.church inika libandla lakho indzawo lephakeme yekufinyelela kucuketfwe lokwabelana ngako libandla lakho. Kuya ngekutsi libandla lakho likulungisele ini, ungaba nekufinyelela ekushumayeleni, kusakazwa lokuphilako, kufundza liBhayibheli, ivesi lemihla ngemihla, netincwadzi tetifundvo -- konkhe kusuka ngekhatsi kwe-member portal.

</div>

## Tici Tekucuketfwe Letitfolakalako

### Tishumayelo (Sermons)

Bhrawuza futsi ubukele tishumayelo letichuywe kusuka ebandleni lakho. Umtapo wetishumayelo webandla lakho uhleleke ngeku-playlist ne-series, lokwenta kube lula kutfola imilayezo yakadzeni.

### Kusakazwa Lokuphilako (Live Streaming)

Bukela kusakaza lokuphilako kwebandla lakho ngco ku-B1.church. Nangabe inkhonzo iphila, ungajoyina lokusakazwako futsi uhlanganyele nalabanye lababukelako nge-chat.

### LiBhayibheli

Fundza umBhalo usebentisa i-Bible reader lesakhiwe ngekhatsi. Ungakhona kuhamba etincwadzini, tehluko, netivesi ngaphandle kwekushiya sayithi.

### Ivesi Lelanga (Verse of the Day)

Buka sitfombe sevesi leliBhayibheli lesakhiwe ngalokuhle lesiguquka mihla yonkhe. Sitfombe sitiguqula ngokutenzekela kute sihambelane nesikrini sakho.

### Tifundvo (Lessons)

Buka tifundvo letihlelekile kusuka etincwadzini tebandla lakho. Nangabe ungutitjhela nome umholi, ungasebentisa **Teacher View** kute ubone tifundvo ngendlela yesikrini lesigcwele, lelula kuyihamba, lefanele kufundzisa nome kwetfula.

### Tifundvo (Sekuyintfo Lengasasetjentiswa - Tab Lefakiwe)

I-tab **Lessons** lefakiwe ngekhatsi seyintfo lengasasetjentiswa (deprecated). Kudlala kwekharikhulamu manje kutfolakala ku-**[FreePlay](/docs/freeplay/)**, i-app ye-TV yamahhala ye-ChurchApps yemakilasi. Kute ubhrawuze ikharikhulamu ngefoni, ithableti, nome ikhompyutha, ngena (sign in) ku-[Lessons.church](https://lessons.church) ngco. Bona likhasi [le-Lessons reference](./lessons.md) kute utfole imininingwane.

## Kutfola Kucuketfwe

Emakhasi ekucuketfwe atfolakala nge-navigation yesayithi lebandla lakho. Umphatsi webandla lakho ukhetsa kutsi ngutiphi tici tekucuketfwe letivunyelwe nekutsi tibonakala njani ku-menu. Nangabe ungasiboni sinye sesici sekucuketfwe lesibalulwe ngenhla, kungenteka asikalungiselwa libandla lakho.

:::info
[Tishumayelo](./sermons.md) ne[liBhayibheli](./bible.md) tiyatfolakala emphakatsini wonkhe. Letinye tici kungenteka tidzinga kutsi [ungenile](../getting-started/logging-in.md).
:::
