# Imihlahlandlela Yemavolontiya

<div class="article-intro">

Sifundzo sonkhe ku-Lessons.church sihlanganisa imihlahlandlela lelungiselelwe tindzima letitsite tevolontiya. Kunekunika ivolontiya nga inye lonkhe luhlelo lwesifundzo, ungayikhombisa umhlahlandlela lokhombisa kuphela imiyalo lehambelana nendzima yayo, kugcina tintfo tilula futsi ticondzile.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekutsi Ucale</h4>

- Condza sakhiwo sesifundzo ngekufundza [Kubuka Tifundzo](../browsing/viewing-lessons.md)
- Yiya kusifundzo lofuna kusilungiselela imihlahlandlela

</div>

## Kutsi Imihlahlandlela Ihlelwe Njani

Tifundzo tihlukaniswe **tincenye** (sibonelo, "Kwemukela", "Indzaba YeLibhayibheli", "Umsebenti Weliqembu Lelincane"). Ngekhatsi kwencenye ngayinye, **tindzima** tinikwa **tento** letitsite. Kunetinhlobo letine tetento:

- **Sho** -- Umbhalo lohlelwe kutsi ivolontiya iwufundze nobe iwuchaze ngemavi ayo.
- **Yenta** -- Sento nobe umsebenti lokufanele wentiwe.
- **Chaza** -- Imininingwane yesisekelo nobe tikhumbuto tevolontiya (akukhulunywa ngayo ngelizwi).
- **Dlala** -- Ividiyo nobe umsindvo lokufanele udlalwe kuliqembu.

Nangabe ivolontiya ivula umhlahlandlela wayo, ibona incenye ngayinye ngekulandzelana, kunetento letinikwe indzima yayo kuphela. Loku kugcina tintfo tilula futsi ticondzile.

## Kubuka Umhlahlandlela Wevolontiya

1. Vula sifundzo lofuna kusilungiselela.
2. Fune luhlu lwe **tindzima** letihlanganiswe nendzawo yesifundzo.
3. Chofota libito lendzima kute uvule umhlahlandlela waloyo mdzimo.

Umhlahlandlela ukhombisa incenye ngayinye njengemgudvu wenyatselo-nga-nyatselo. Emavolontiya angahamba kuwo efownini, ku-tablet, nobe kukhompyutha ngesikhatsi seklasi.

## Kuphrinta Imihlahlandlela

Nangabe emavolontiya akho atsandza likhophi lelibhaliwe, ungaphrinta nawuphi umhlahlandlela ngco kusukela ku-brawuza.

1. Vula umhlahlandlela wendzima lofunako.
2. Sebentisa umsebenti we-brawuza yakho we **Phrinta** (**Ctrl+P** nobe **Cmd+P**).
3. Phrinta likhasi nobe ulingcine njenge-PDF kute ulisakatise ngesikhatsi lesitako.

:::tip
Yabelana ngetihlanganisi temihlahlandlela nemavolontiya akho kusenesikhatsi kuze bakwati kuhlola imiyalo yabo ngaphambi keklasi. Kufundza lokushesha ngeliviki kusita wonkhe umuntfu kutiva alungile kakhudlwana ngeSonto ekuseni.
:::

## Tindzima Letinyenti

Letinye tifundzo tinetindzima letinyenti -- sibonelo, titjeli lesikhulu, umholi weliqembu lelincane, kanye nevolontiya letekhono. Indzima ngayinye inomhlahlandlela wayo, ngako ungaba nemihlahlandlela lekhetsekile njengobe sifundzo sikudzinga.

:::info
Nangabe indzima ingasebenti kulelikilasi lakho, ungayifihla usebentisa tici te- [kulungiselwa](../customization/customizing-lessons.md) kuze emavolontiya akho abone kuphela tindzima letibalulekile.
:::
