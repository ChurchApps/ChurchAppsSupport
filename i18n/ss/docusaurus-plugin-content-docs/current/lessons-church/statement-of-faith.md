# Sitatimende Selukholo

<div class="article-intro">

Nomanjalo sivuma kutsi asikho sitatimende lesentiwe ngumuntfu lesingaveta ngalokugcwele kucondza kweliBhayibheli kwelukholo lwetfu, sinikeza lesi sitatimende kute sicacise tinkholelo letiyisisekelo te-ChurchApps njengenhlangano. Letinkholelo tichoboza inchubo yekukhetsa nekuhlola konkhe lokucuketfwe lokutfolakala ku-Lessons.church.

</div>

**NKULUNKULU:** Kukhona Nkulunkulu munye longcwele lokhona phakadze ngebantfu labatsatfu -- uYise, iNdvodzana, kanye neMoya Longcwele. Nkulunkulu wadala tonkhe tintfo letibonakalako nalenatibonakalali. Nkulunkulu uphelele ekuhlakanipheni, emandleni, nasetsandzeni, wati konkhe lokwendlulile, lokukhona, nalokutako, futsi luhlelo lwakhe lwekuhlenga lolubusako lwabekwa ngaphambi kwekusekelwa kwemhlaba. (Gen 1:1-2, Dt. 6:4, Heb 11:3, Eph 1:9-10; Semb 13:8)

**JESU:** Jesu Khristu yiNdvodzana yaNkulunkulu yodvwana, latalwa ngunkulukumba, uNkulunkulu ngalokugcwele futsi ngumuntfu ngalokugcwele, futsi nguMsindzisi neNkhosi yetfu. Jesu, lowabengenasono, wafa esikhundleni sekwentela lokwenteka sikhundla sema sono etfu, atfwala lulaka lwaNkulunkulu, futsi wabuyisana naNkulunkulu bonkhe labametsembako. Jesu wavuswa emtimbeni ekunqobeni sono nekufa. Wenyukela esandleni sekudla saYise lapho manje abusa khona njengeNkhosi yetfu, umphristi lomkhulu, nemeli, kuze kubuye kwakhe lokukhatzimulako. (Johane 3:16, Kol 1:15; 2:9-15; 1 Khor 15:3-8, 20-28; 2 Khor 5:18-21; Heb 4:14-15)

**MOYA LONGCWELE:** Moya Longcwele unguNkulunkulu ngalokugcwele futsi usebenta ebandleni nasemhlabeni. Moya Longcwele udvonsa bonkhe bantfu kuKhristu ngekukhanyisa livangeli nangekulahla sono. Moya Longcwele uhlala kuwo umphilo wemuntfu lokholwako kute aguqule, aholele, acinisekise, futsi awuphatse umphilo weBuKhristu lonetitselo. (Johane 16:8-11; Imisebenti 2:38; 2 Khor 3:17-18; Gal 3:2)

**LIBHAYIBHELI:** Nkulunkulu wembulwa eLibhayibhelini, liVi laNkulunkulu leliphefumulelwe ngalokwehlukile futsi lelingephutsi kuko konkhe lelikuchazako. LiBhayibheli ligunya lelisemuva kuto tonkhe tindzaba tekukholwa nekutiphatsa. (2 Thim 3:16; 2 Phet 1:20-21)

**BUNTFU:** Nkulunkulu wadala bonkhe bantfu, lomunye nalomunye, ngemfanekiso wakhe, ngako-ke bonkhe bantfu banelisiko nenhloso lokwakhiwe kubo. Ngesono semuntfu wekucala nemfati wekucala (Adamu na-Eva), kufa kwangena emhlabeni. Bonkhe bonile futsi basilele budvumiseni baNkulunkulu, bahlukanisiwe kuNkulunkulu futsi bangenatsemba ngaphandle kwengati yaJesu Khristu. (Gen 1:26-27; Gen 3; Rom 3:23; Eph 2:1-3)

**INSINDZISO:** Insindziso ingatfolakala kuKhristu kuphela futsi inikwa bonkhe ngemusa ngekukholwa. Lukholo loluphilako lubonakaliswa ngekuphenduka, kuvuma, kubhabhatiswa ngekucwiliswa, kanye nemphilo yekulalela. (Rom 3:23; 5:12, Imisebenti 2:38, Gal 3:26-29; Eph 2:4-10)

**LIBANDLA:** Libandla lingumtimba waKhristu emhlabeni, ngaKhristu njengenhloko. Libandla laNkulunkulu lakhiwe ngubuphristi babo bonkhe labakholwako, labasebenta njengetikhonzi telivangeli ngekwesipho lababenikwe sona nguNkulunkulu. Ndzawonye libandla libitwa kwenta bafundzi kuto tonkhe tive kuze kubuye kwakhe uKhristu. (Mat 28:18-20; Eph 3:10; 4:11-13; Kol 1:18; 1 Phet 2:9-10)

**KUBUYA KWAKHRISTU:** UKhristu utabuya ngalokubonakalako kubuyisela indalo nekwehlulela umhlaba. Kutakuba nekuvuka kwemitimba kubalabo labakholwako impilo lengunaphakadze naNkulunkulu ezulwini kanye nalabangakholwa kwehlulelwa lokungunaphakadze esihogweni. Ezulwini, sono singeke sabakhona futsi labo labakuKhristu batawuphila ndzawonye naNkulunkulu kuze kube phakadze. (Imisebenti 1:11; 2 Thes 1:5-12; 1 Thes 4:13-18; Semb 20:11-15)

## Tikhombo Tetifundzo

Lessons.church isebentisa tifundzo letivela kuto tonkhe tinhlobo letahlukene temitfombo yebuKhristu. Sonkhe sifundzo lesisinikezako sihlolwa ngekuhlolwa kwetfu kwangekhatsi kute kucaciswe kutfobeka kwelibhayibheli nekucondza kwemfundziso. Siyacondza kutsi kunetibonelo letahlukene ngekhatsi kweLibandla, futsi sikhetsa kuhlangana kuloko lesikwabelana ngako. Imininingwane mayelana nebalobi balesifundzo ngasinye ingatfolwa encenyeni ye-"Ngathi" yetifundzo letitsite.

Sikhutsata bantfu labasebentisa lenchubo kutsi basitsintse mayelana nemibuto labanayo ngesifundzo nobe uchungechunge lolutsite.
