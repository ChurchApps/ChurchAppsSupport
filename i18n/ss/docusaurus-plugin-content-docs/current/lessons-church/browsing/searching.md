# Kusesha

<div class="article-intro">

Nawati sihloko lofuna kufundzisa ngaso kodvwa ungaciniseki kutsi ngeluphi luhlelo nome sifundvo lesicutsa sona, sici sekusesha sikusita utfole kontenti lefanele ngekushesha. Kusesha kusebenta kuwo wonkhe umtapo we-Lessons.church, kuhlanganisa kontenti levela kubanikeli bangaphandle.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Tijwayeze sifingqo se-[Kuphequlula Kontenti](./index.md) kuze ucondzisise tonkhe tindlela tekuhamba letitfolakalako

</div>

## Kusesha Kanjani

1. Yaphuma uye **kukhasi lekusesha** losetulu kwesayithi le-Lessons.church.
2. Bhala ligama lekusesha nome umusho **ebhokisini lekusesha** (sibonelo, "kutsetselelana," "David naGoliyathi," nome "umthandazo").
3. Cindzetela **Enter** nome uchofote inkinombho yekusesha kubona imiphumela yakho.

## Indlela Kusesha Lokusebenta Ngayo

Kusesha kusekelwe kusesho lolugcwele kuwo wonkhe umtapo we-Lessons.church. Kukhangela kuletihloko tetifundzo, tincazelo, nakukontenti kutfola lokufanako nemagama akho ekusesha. Imiphumela ihlanganisa tifundzo letivela kuto tonkhe tinhlelo netabanikeli letitfolakalako, ngako-ke utfola umbono lophelele waloko lokutfolakalako ngesihloko lesitsite.

## Kuhamba Emiphumeleni

Imiphumela yekusesha ikhombisa tifundzo letifanako kanye nemininingwane yekutsi ngusiphi luhlelo nesifundvo lesisodvwa nesisodvwa singesalo. Chofota nawuphi umphumela kuze uye ngco ekhasini lalesosifundzo, lapho ungabona khona kontenti lephelele, tigaba, tindzima, nemibhalo langalandwa.

## Nini Kusetjentiswa Kusesha

Kusesha kusiza kakhulu nangabe:

- Unendzaba yeliBhayibheli lesitsite nome sihloko lesitsite ecinjeni sakho futsi ufuna kubona konkhe kufundzisa lokutfolakalako lokucutsa yona.
- Uhlela tichungechunge letikhetsekile futsi udzinga kutfola tifundzo ngesihloko lesitsite.
- Awukajwayelani nato tonkhe tinhlelo letitfolakalako futsi ufuna indlela lensha yekuhlola kutsi kutfolakala njani.

:::tip
Nangabe ligama lakho lekusesha likhipha imiphumela leminyenti kakhulu, zama umusho lochaza ngco. Sibonelo, sesha "Good Samaritan" kunekutsi ku "Samaritan" kuze kuncipha imiphumela.
:::

Nangabe sewusati luhlelo lofuna, kuphequlula ngeluhlelo kuvamise kuba kusheshe. Bona [Tinhlelo Netifundvo](./programs-and-studies.md) kutfola imininingwane yekuhamba sakhiwo sekontenti.
