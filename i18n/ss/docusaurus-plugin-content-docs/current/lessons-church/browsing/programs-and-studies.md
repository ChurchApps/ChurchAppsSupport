# Tinhlelo Netifundvo

<div class="article-intro">

Konkhe kufundzisa ku-Lessons.church kuhleliwe ngendlela lecacile. Kucondzisisa lesisakhiwo kutakusita utfole kontenti lefanele libandla lakho ngekushesha futsi uhambe emkhatsini wetifundzo letihlobene ngekulula.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Tijwayeze sifingqo se-[Kuphequlula Kontenti](./index.md) kuze ucondzisise tindlela letahlukene tekutfola kontenti

</div>

## Sakhiwo Sekontenti

Kontenti ihleliwe kusukela lokubanti kuya lokutsite:

- **Umnikeli** -- Inhlangano leshicilela kufundzisa (Lessons.church nome umnikeli wangaphandle).
- **Luhlelo** -- Sicu sekufundzisa, njengekutsi "Ark Kids" nome "NextLevel." Luhlelo ngalunye siciniso setifundvo letihlobene letakhelwe ngesihloko nome licembu leminyaka.
- **Sifundvo** -- Lilinga ngekhatsi kwelinhlelo, njengekutsi "The Good Samaritan." Sifundvo ngasinye siba nesicu setifundzo letifanele tifundziswe ndzawonye, kanyendlula emavikini lamanyenti.
- **Sifundzo** -- Lingeniso lekufundzisa lelilinye ngekhatsi kwesifundvo. Sifundzo ngasinye sihlanganisa tigaba, tindzima, tento, nemibhalo langalandwa.
- **I-Venue** -- Luhlobo lwesifundzo lolwakhelwe licembu leminyaka, njengekutsi Preschool nome Elementary. Ema-venue akuvumela kutsi lesifundzo lesifanako sicondziswe kutilaleli letahlukene.

## Kuphequlula Tinhlelo

Kutfola tinhlelo:

1. Vakashela likhasi lasekhaya le-Lessons.church.
2. Skrola uhambe kuluhlu lwetinhlelo letitfolakalako nome ubuke tinhlelo letikhangelwe letisetulu.
3. Chofota **ligama lelinhlelo** nome sitfombe salo kuvula likhasi lelinhlelo.

Likhasi lelinhlelo ngalinye lihlanganisa:

- **Incazelo** -- Umbono waloko kufundzisa lokucutsako nekutsi kwakhelwe bani.
- **Sitfombe selinhlelo** -- Simo lesibonakalako setichungechunge.
- **Vidiyo yekukhangisa** -- Letinye tinhlelo tihlanganisa vidiyo lensane yekubonisa kuze ubone sitayela nebunono bekontenti ngaphambi kwekutibopha kuyo.
- **Tifundvo** -- Luhlu lwato tonkhe tifundvo elinhlelweni, letingahle tihlelwe tibe tinhlobo.

## Kuphequlula Tifundvo

Kusukela ekhasini lelinhlelo:

1. Phequlula luhlu lwetifundvo. Nangabe luhlelo luhlela tifundvo tibe tinhlobo, utawutibona tihlanganiswe ngaleyondlela.
2. Chofota **ligama lesifundvo** kuvula likhasi lesifundvo.
3. Likhasi lesifundvo likhombisa tonkhe tifundzo letifakwe kulesosifundvo, tihleliwe ngeluhlelo lwekufundzisa.

Kusukela lapha, ungakwati kungena kunome ngusiphi sifundzo kubona kontenti yaso lephelele, kubandzakanya tigaba, tindzima, nemibhalo langalandwa. Bona [Kubuka Tifundzo](./viewing-lessons.md) kutfola indlela lecacile.

:::info
Tinhlelo netifundvo tiphatswa ngabanikeli bekufundzisa. Nangabe ungumchami wekontenti, ungaphatsa tinhlelo netifundvo takho ngesigaba se-[Kuphatsa](../admin/index.md).
:::
