# Tinhlelo Netifundvo

<div class="article-intro">

Sisebenti sekuhlola tifundzo sisebentisa inchubo lelandzelana nyatselo ngenyatselo kuze kuncishiswe kufike ekucuketfweni lokufanele lofuna kukudlala. Sinyatselo ngasinye sibonisa luhlu lwemakhadi langu-3 wemakholomu enetitfombe nesihloko, lokwenta kube lula kuhamba kuyo yonkhe inchubomlomo yetifundzo nge-remote yakho ye-TV.

</div>

<div class="prereqs">
<h4>Ngaphambi Kwekucala</h4>

- Cinisekisa kutsi TV yakho inelusix humano lwe-internete (kuyadzingeka kuze kuhlolwe luhlu lwetinhlelo)
- Hamba uye esikrinini se-Browse Programs usebentisa incenye yaseceleni

</div>

## Kuhlola Tinhlelo

1. Vula sikrini se-Browse Programs
2. Tinhlelo tibonakala njengeluhlu lwemakhadi anetitfombe, matsatfu ngekubhalwa
3. Sebentisa i-pad yenkambo ye-remote yakho kuze ukhombise luhlelo
4. Cindzetela **Select** kuze uluvule

Likhadi ngalinye leluhlelo libonisa sitfombe sesembozo saloluhlelo. Uma kute sitfombe lesitfolakalako, kubonakala likhadi lelijwayelekile.

## Kukhetsa Sifundvo

Ngemuva kwekukhetsa luhlelo, sikrini se-Select a Study siyalayisha sinato tonkhe tifundvo talolo hlelo.

1. Emakhadi etifundvo abonakala eluhlwini lwemakholomu langu-3 anetitfombe tesembozo namagama etifundvo
2. Hamba uye esifundveni losifunako bese ucindzetela **Select**
3. Cindzetela **Back** kuze ubuyele eluhlwini lwetinhlelo

## Kukhetsa Sifundzo

Ngemuva kwekukhetsa sifundvo, sikrini se-Select a Lesson sibonisa tonkhe tifundzo talesosifundvo.

1. Emakhadi etifundzo abonisa titfombe tesembozo nemagama etifundzo
2. Hamba uye esifundzweni losifunako bese ucindzetela **Select**
3. Cindzetela **Back** kuze ubuyele eluhlwini lwetifundvo

## Kukhetsa Indzawo

Sikrini seminingwane yesifundzo sibonisa ligama lesifundzo, sihloko, nencazelo, konkhe kuembelwe etulu kwesitfombe sesembozo sesifundzo. Ngaphansi kwencazelo, utawubona inkhinobho yinye kumbe letinyenti tetindzawo.

Tindzawo timele tinhlobo letehlukene tesifundzo lesifananako. Sibonelo, sifundzo singaba netindzawo letehlukene "Bantfwana Labancane," "Emabanga Aphansi," ne "Emabanga Aphakathi." Indzawo ngayinye ifaka emafayela emidiya lafanele leso simo.

1. Sebentisa i-pad yenkambo kuze ukhombise indzawo loyifunako
2. Cindzetela **Select** kuze kucale kulayishwa kwekucuketfwe
3. FreePlay ilanda luhlu lwekudlala lwendzawo bese icalisa umdlali

:::tip
Uma ubona inkhinobho yinye yendzawo nje, kusuke kunenhlobo yinye yesifundzo letfolakalako. Yikhetse kuze kucale kudlala ngalokushesha.
:::

## Kuhamba Emuva

Nome ngukuphi lapho ku-inchubo yekuhlola, cindzetela **Back** ku-remote yakho kuze ubuyele ebangeni lelandzulele:

- Kusuka esikrinini seminingwane yesifundzo, **Back** ibuyisela eluhlwini lwetifundzo
- Kusuka eluhlwini lwetifundzo, **Back** ibuyisela eluhlwini lwetifundvo
- Kusuka eluhlwini lwetifundvo, **Back** ibuyisela eluhlwini lwetinhlelo
- Kusuka eluhlwini lwetinhlelo, **Back** ivula incenye yaseceleni
