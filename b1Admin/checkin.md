### Step 1: Install the Check-in App
Install the [B1.church Checkin App](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=church.b1.checkin) on your Android tablet.

### Step 2: Configure Your Printer
Follow the [printer setup instructions](https://support.brother.com/g/b/downloadtop.aspx?c=us&lang=en&prod=lpql1110nwbeus&_ga=2.156293496.295918436.1665227103-2042905656.1665227103&_gl=1*6hne9l*_ga*MjA0MjkwNTY1Ni4xNjY1MjI3MTAz*_ga_NCEW43SJ8W*MTY2NTIyNzEwMy4xLjEuMTY2NTIyNzE0MS4yMi4wLjA.) to connect your printer to the same WiFi network as your tablet.

**Need help?** Watch this [printer setup tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U5pmTRddt2w).

### Step 3: Connect and Test
Follow this video tutorial to connect your printer to the app and print your first name tag:
